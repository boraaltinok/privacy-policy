## Common Issues and Solutions

### App Performance
**Problem**: App is slow or freezing
**Solution**: 
1. Close and restart the app
2. Restart your device
3. Update to the latest app version
4. Free up device storage space
5. Check your internet connection

**Problem**: Recipes not loading
**Solution**:
1. Check internet connectivity
2. Try switching between WiFi and mobile data
3. Clear app cache (Android: Settings > Apps > Airfryer Rezepte > Storage > Clear Cache)
4. Restart the app

### Recipe and Cooking Issues
**Problem**: Recipe results don't match expectations
**Solution**:
- Verify your airfryer model supports the temperature range
- Check ingredient measurements and preparation
- Adjust cooking time based on your specific airfryer model
- Consider food thickness and quantity variations
- Contact support for recipe-specific guidance

**Problem**: Nutrition information seems incorrect
**Solution**:
- Nutrition values are estimates based on standard ingredients
- Actual values may vary with ingredient brands and preparation
- Use nutrition information as a general guide, not medical advice
- Consult nutritionists for specific dietary requirements

### Account and Data
**Problem**: Lost favorites or cooking history
**Solution**:
1. Ensure you're using the same device
2. Check if you're logged into the correct Apple/Google account
3. Verify your subscription status
4. Contact support for data recovery assistance

**Problem**: Subscription issues
**Solution**:
1. Verify purchase through App Store/Google Play receipts
2. Check subscription status in device settings
3. Restore purchases within the app (Settings > Restore Purchases)
4. Contact support with purchase receipt for manual restoration

## Troubleshooting Guide

### Login and Authentication
**Issue**: Can't access premium features
1. Verify active subscription in device settings
2. Tap "Restore Purchases" in app settings

4. Contact support if issue persists

### Recipe Filtering
**Issue**: Filters not working properly
1. Clear all filters and reapply one at a time
2. Check for app updates in your app store
3. Restart the app to refresh filter system

### Cooking Mode
**Issue**: Timers not working correctly
1. Ensure notification permissions are enabled
2. Keep app open during cooking for best timer performance
3. Use device's built-in timer as backup

## Food Safety and Cooking Tips

### Airfryer Safety
- Always preheat your airfryer as specified in recipes
- Don't overcrowd the cooking basket for even results
- Use food thermometer to verify internal cooking temperatures
- Clean airfryer regularly according to manufacturer instructions

### General Food Safety
- Wash hands before and after handling food
- Use separate cutting boards for meat and vegetables
- Store leftovers properly and consume within 3-4 days
- Follow "when in doubt, throw it out" rule for food safety

### Recipe Modifications
- Adjust cooking times for different airfryer models
- Scale ingredients proportionally when changing serving sizes
- Consider altitude adjustments for cooking times and temperatures
- Test doneness before serving, especially with meat and poultry
