## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### How do I add the Notify Me widget to my product pages?
1. Go to **Online Store → Themes → Customize** in your Shopify admin.
2. Navigate to a product page template.
3. Click **Add block** and search for "Notify Me Button" under the App blocks section.
4. Position it where you'd like it to appear (below the Add to Cart button is recommended).
5. Customize the button colors, text, and placeholder to match your theme.
6. Save your changes.

### How do I add the Low Stock Badge?
Follow the same steps as above, but search for "Low Stock Badge" instead. You can configure the stock threshold and customize the badge colors in the theme editor.

### When do customers see the Notify Me form?
The form only appears when the currently selected variant is **out of stock**. If the product has inventory available, the form stays hidden and the normal Add to Cart button shows instead.

### How are notifications sent?
When Shopify detects an inventory update (restock) for a product, our app automatically checks for pending subscribers and sends them a notification email. This happens in real-time — no manual action required.

### Can I customize the notification email?
Yes! Go to **Settings** in the app to customize:
- The sender name (e.g., "Your Store Name")
- The email subject line (supports `{product}` and `{variant}` placeholders)
- You can preview the email live and send a test email to yourself.

### Can I send emails from my own domain?
Yes. In **Settings → Send from your own domain (advanced)**, you can connect your own [Resend](https://resend.com) account. This lets you send notification emails from your own domain (e.g., notifications@yourstore.com) for better deliverability.

### What is the subscriber limit on the Free plan?
The Free plan supports up to 20 active subscribers. All features are included — there are no feature restrictions. When you need more capacity, upgrade to the Starter plan ($9/month) for unlimited subscribers.

### What happens when I uninstall the app?
All your data is **permanently deleted** when you uninstall the app, including subscriber records, notification logs, and settings. This action cannot be undone.

### How do I request deletion of my data as a customer?
If you're a customer who signed up for a back-in-stock notification and want your data deleted, please email us at [support@nextline.software](mailto:support@nextline.software) with the email address you used and the store name. We'll delete your records promptly.
