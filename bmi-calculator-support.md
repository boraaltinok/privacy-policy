## Getting Started

### How do I calculate my BMI?
1. Open the Application
2. Enter your age, sex, height, and weight on the BMI Input screen
3. Tap the Calculate button
4. The Application will display your BMI value and category

The BMI calculator is free, unlimited, and works in metric (cm/kg) or imperial (ft/in, lbs) units. You can toggle units from the BMI Input screen.

### What does my BMI category mean?
The Application shows one of the standard BMI categories (Underweight, Healthy, Above Healthy Range / Overweight, or Obese). For ages 2–19, BMI is interpreted differently than adult BMI; the Application uses child / teen-safe explanatory copy. BMI for children and teens should be interpreted with age, sex, growth, and professional guidance in mind. In all cases, BMI is a screening indicator, not a medical diagnosis — please see the in-app "BMI is a starting point" explanation and the Health and BMI Disclaimer in the Terms & Conditions.

### Can I use the Application without creating an account?
Yes. The Application does not ask you to sign up, register, choose a username, or provide an email address. An anonymous identifier is assigned automatically so that your data persists across sessions on the same device.

### What's included with Premium?
A paid subscription or Lifetime purchase unlocks:

- **Three swap actions per card:** "I'll try this", "Already do this", "Replace"

### How does the AI Coach work?
After purchase, open the Coach tab, then either tap a suggested question chip or type your own message. The Coach uses your plan context (goal, struggles, coaching style preference) to generate a personalized reply. The Coach is for general informational support — please review the Terms & Conditions Health and BMI Disclaimer before acting on any advice.

### Why does my plan look different from someone else's?
The AI-generated Action Plan uses your individual BMI inputs and your answers to the brief in-app interview (goal direction, struggles, daily routine, coaching style). Plans are personalized per user and can be refreshed at any time using the Check In & Refresh Plan flow.

### How do I manage or cancel my subscription?
To manage or cancel a subscription on iOS:
1. Open the **Settings** app on your device
2. Tap your **Apple ID** at the top
3. Tap **Subscriptions**
4. Select **BMI Calculator: Weight Plan**
5. Choose to modify or cancel

Cancellation must be done at least 24 hours before the end of the current period to avoid the next charge.

### How do I restore my purchase on a new device?
Open the Application's paywall screen and tap **Restore**. Make sure you are signed in to the same Apple ID that you used to make the purchase. Your entitlement will be restored to the new installation.

### How do I get a refund?
Refund requests are handled directly by the Apple App Store and not by the Service Provider. Please request a refund at [reportaproblem.apple.com](https://reportaproblem.apple.com).

## Troubleshooting

### My Plan isn't loading after I subscribed
1. Make sure you are connected to the internet
2. Force-quit the Application and reopen it
3. Open the paywall and tap **Restore** to re-sync your entitlement
4. If the Plan still does not appear after a minute, contact support@nextline.software. The Application includes an automatic fallback for AI generation issues, so most plan-generation problems should resolve automatically — but subscription, entitlement, or network issues may still require support.

### My BMI category looks wrong
Double-check your height, weight, age, sex, and unit-system inputs on the BMI Input screen. For ages 2–19, BMI is interpreted differently than for adults — please review the result alongside age and sex context, and where in doubt consult a parent, guardian, or healthcare professional.

### The AI Coach is not responding
Make sure you are connected to the internet. If the issue persists, contact support@nextline.software — the AI Coach depends on third-party AI infrastructure and very occasionally has elevated latency.

### Reset BMI history or weight log
The Application stores your history locally and in the cloud under an anonymous identifier. Use the **Delete my data** option in the Application's profile/settings screen to remove your cloud-stored data. To clear local data as well, uninstall and reinstall the Application.
