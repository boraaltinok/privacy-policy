## 1. Information Collection

When you use Card Value Scanner for Pokémon ("the App"), operated by NEXTLINE YAZILIM LİMİTED ŞİRKETİ, we may collect and process the following types of information:

### 1.1 Image Data
- **Card Images**: When you use our card scanning features, the app processes photos you take or select from your device. These images are compressed, converted to base64 format, and transmitted securely via HTTPS to Firebase Functions for AI-powered card identification using OpenRouter services.
- **Processed Images**: Images are processed to identify Pokémon cards, including card variants, conditions, and pricing information.
- **Retention**: Card images are processed in real-time and are not permanently stored on our servers. Images may be temporarily retained in memory during the identification process and are deleted after processing completion.

### 1.2 Device and Usage Information
- **Device Identifiers**: We collect anonymous device identifiers for analytics and crash reporting purposes.
- **App Usage Data**: Information about how you interact with the app, including features used, session duration, and navigation patterns.
- **Camera Permissions**: The app requires camera access to capture card images for identification purposes.

### 1.3 Collection and Pricing Data
- **Local Collections**: Card collection data is stored locally on your device using Hive database. This data is not transmitted to external servers.
- **Pricing Requests**: When requesting current card pricing, we may log the card identifiers and pricing sources accessed for service improvement.


## 2. Third Party Access

Firebase may collect additional data as described in Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
