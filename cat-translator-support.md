# Cat Translator: Meow & Talk - Support

Welcome to the Cat Translator support page. We're here to help you get the most out of your two-way cat-communication experience. Cat Translator is made **just for fun** — enjoy it as a playful way to bond with your cat.

## Getting Started

Cat Translator lets you record your cat's meow for a playful interpretation, talk back with real bundled cat sounds, and discover which sounds your cat seems to respond to. The app is compatible with iOS and Android devices.

### Key Features
- **Cat → Human**: Record a meow, pick the context, and get a playful mood, possible meanings, and a first-person line.
- **Human → Cat**: Pick a phrase and play a real bundled cat sound, then log your cat's reaction.
- **Insights**: Over time, see which sounds your cat responds best to (premium).
- **History**: Your decodes, talks, and reactions are saved locally on your device.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General Questions

**Q: How do I get started with Cat Translator?**
A: Download the app from your device's app store, allow microphone access when prompted, create a quick cat profile, and you're ready to record your first meow.

**Q: Is this a real translation of what my cat is saying?**
A: No. Cat Translator is an entertainment app. The moods, meanings, and lines are playful interpretations generated for fun — they are not a scientific or veterinary translation. For any health or behavior concerns, please consult a veterinarian.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes. Cat Translator is a local-first application — your translation history, talk history, cat profiles, and preferences are stored on your device only and are not transmitted to our servers. Analytics and crash data sent to third-party services are transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS). For more information, refer to our [Privacy Policy](https://github.com/boraaltinok/privacy-policy/blob/main/cat-translator-privacy-policy.md).

### Microphone & Permissions

**Q: Why does Cat Translator need microphone access?**
A: The microphone is used to record your cat's meow so the app can produce a playful interpretation. **Raw audio is never uploaded or stored on our servers** — the recording stays on your device, and only a few non-identifying numeric loudness values may be used to generate the interpretation.

**Q: The waveform stays flat or there is no reading. What should I do?**
A: Make sure you have granted microphone permission. Go to your device Settings > Cat Translator > Microphone and ensure it is enabled, then make sure no other app is using the microphone.

**Q: Do I need an internet connection?**
A: No. Recording, the on-device interpretation, playing bundled cat sounds, and your saved history all work offline. An internet connection is only used for subscriptions, analytics, crash reporting, and the optional online interpretation service when available.

### Talking to Your Cat

**Q: How does "Talk to your cat" work?**
A: Pick a phrase and the app plays a real, pre-recorded cat sound from its bundled library. Afterward you can log how your cat reacted to help surface "responds best to" insights.

**Q: The "Say it yourself" speech option asks me to pick a phrase instead. Why?**
A: The speak-your-own path needs the online service to be available. When it isn't reachable, simply use the phrase chips — they are the core offline way to talk back.

### Subscription & Premium

**Q: What do I get with Premium?**
A: Premium unlocks unlimited decodes and talks, multiple cat profiles, full history, and "responds best to" insights.

**Q: How do I restore my purchase on a new device?**
A: Go to Settings > Restore Purchases. Your subscription is linked to your App Store or Google Play account and will be restored automatically.

**Q: How do I cancel my subscription?**
A: Subscriptions are managed through your App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android) account. Go to your device's subscription settings to cancel. Deleting the app does not cancel a subscription.

## Data Management

**Q: How do I delete my data?**
A: Your translation history, talk history, and cat profiles are stored locally on your device. You can delete them by clearing the app's data in your device settings, or by uninstalling the Application. Data collected by third-party services (analytics, crash reports) is subject to their respective retention policies as described in our [Privacy Policy](https://github.com/boraaltinok/privacy-policy/blob/main/cat-translator-privacy-policy.md).
