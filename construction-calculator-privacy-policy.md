# Privacy Policy

**Privacy Policy**

This privacy policy applies to the Construction Calculator: Feet Inch app (hereby referred to as "Application") for mobile devices that was created by NEXTLINE YAZILIM LİMİTED ŞİRKETİ (hereby referred to as "Service Provider") as a Commercial service. This service is intended for use "AS IS".

**No Account and Offline-First Design**

The Application is a fully offline construction calculator. It does **not** require you to create an account, sign up, register, log in, choose a username, or provide an email address or password. There is **no user profile**, and none of the calculations, measurements, saved projects, or history you create in the Application are uploaded to, or stored on, the Service Provider's servers — the Application has no such server or backend for your content. All of the measurement work you do stays on your device (see "Local Device Storage" below).

**Information Collection and Use**

The Application collects a limited amount of information automatically when you download and use it, primarily for analytics, crash diagnostics, remote configuration, and in-app purchase management. This information may include information such as

*   Your device's Internet Protocol address (e.g. IP address)
*   The type of mobile device you use and its operating system version
*   Crash diagnostic data when the Application encounters an error
*   Product analytics events (such as which screens you view, which tools you open, and which features you interact with)
*   Device identifiers used by the analytics and crash-reporting services described under "Third Party Access" below
*   In-app purchase entitlement status provided by RevenueCat and the Apple App Store, needed to determine whether premium ("Pro") features are available on your installation

The Application does **not** request access to the iOS or Android location APIs and does not use GPS, Wi-Fi-based, or cellular-tower-based location services. Some third-party services listed under "Third Party Access" below (notably Amplitude and Firebase) may derive an approximate (city / region level) location from the IP address attached to network requests for analytics purposes; the Service Provider does not receive precise geographical coordinates from your device.

The Application does **not** access the microphone, camera, photo library, contacts, calendar, motion sensors, or any health data store on your device (such as Apple Health or Google Fit). The Application does **not** ask you to provide any personal contact information.

**Data You Enter Into the Application (Stays On Your Device)**

The Application is a calculator, so you enter measurements and related values in order to compute results. This includes, for example:

*   Dimensions and values you type into the calculator and the trade tools (feet, inches, fractions, metric measurements, pitches, spans, quantities, unit costs, and similar inputs)
*   Calculations you save to the running "tape" / history and any named projects you create
*   Your settings and preferences (unit system, fraction resolution, and theme)

This information is created and used **entirely on your device** to perform calculations, draw the on-screen diagrams, and let you review past work. It is **not** transmitted to the Service Provider, is **not** synced to any cloud, and is not associated with your identity. It is not "collected" by the Service Provider in the sense of being sent off your device.

The Service Provider does **not** collect your email address, phone number, real name, postal address, payment card information, or any other directly identifying personal contact information. Payments are processed entirely by the Apple App Store and the Service Provider receives no payment card or billing details.

**Local Device Storage**

The Application stores the following information locally on your device (using an on-device database) so that it can function fully offline and across sessions:

*   Your calculator entries and most recent results
*   Your saved tape / calculation history and any named projects (Pro feature)
*   Your unit-system preference (imperial feet-inch or metric)
*   Your fraction-resolution preference (e.g. 1/16)
*   Your theme preference (system, light, or dark)
*   A first-run / onboarding-completed flag
*   A cached copy of your last-known purchase entitlement, so the Application can recognize your Pro access while offline

This local data is not transmitted off your device by the Application. You can remove it at any time by deleting your saved history within the Application or by uninstalling the Application.

**Third Party Access**

Only the automatically collected diagnostic, usage, and purchase information described above is transmitted to external services, and only to operate analytics, crash reporting, remote configuration, and in-app purchases. The measurements and projects you enter are **not** part of this. The Application uses the following third-party services, each of which has its own privacy policy regarding the handling of data:

*   [Google Analytics for Firebase](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy) — product/usage analytics
*   [Firebase Crashlytics](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy/) — crash diagnostic reporting
*   [Firebase Remote Config](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy) — remote feature flags and paywall configuration (no personal data required)
*   [Amplitude](https://amplitude.com/privacy) — product analytics, such as screen views, feature interactions, subscription funnel events, and approximate location derived from IP address
*   [RevenueCat](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy) — in-app purchase and subscription entitlement management, including purchase attribution (such as Apple's AdServices / Apple Search Ads attribution token) to measure the source of a purchase
*   [Apple App Store](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/) — payment processing and subscription billing

The Service Provider may disclose Automatically Collected Information:

*   as required by law, such as to comply with a subpoena, or similar legal process;
*   when they believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect their rights, protect your safety or the safety of others, investigate fraud, or respond to a government request;
*   with their trusted services providers who work on their behalf, do not have an independent use of the information we disclose to them, and have agreed to adhere to the rules set forth in this privacy statement.

**No Advertising and No Cross-App Tracking**

The Application does **not** display advertisements and does **not** integrate any third-party advertising network. The Application does **not** ask for permission to track you and does **not** present Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt, because it does not track you across apps or websites owned by other companies for advertising purposes. The RevenueCat integration described above may use Apple's AdServices attribution token to measure whether a purchase originated from an Apple Search Ads campaign; this is standard purchase attribution and is not cross-app IDFA tracking.

**Opt-Out Rights**

You can stop all collection of information by the Application easily by uninstalling it. You may use the standard uninstall processes as may be available as part of your mobile device or via the mobile application marketplace or network.

**Data Retention Policy**

The calculations, history, projects, and settings you create are retained locally on your device until you delete them or uninstall the Application; the Service Provider does not hold this data. Automatically collected analytics and crash-diagnostic data is retained by the third-party services above in accordance with their own retention practices. If you have any questions about data handling, please contact the Service Provider at support@nextline.software and they will respond in a reasonable time.

**Children**

The Application is a general-purpose construction and measurement utility. It is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. The Application does not ask any user to provide personal information. If you are a parent or guardian and you believe the Application has been used in a way that concerns you, please contact the Service Provider at support@nextline.software so that any necessary actions can be taken.

**Security**

The Service Provider is concerned about safeguarding the confidentiality of your information. Because the measurements and projects you create stay on your device, the primary safeguard for that content is your device's own security. For the limited automatically collected analytics, crash-diagnostic, and purchase information that is transmitted, data is sent to the third-party services above over encrypted, industry-standard HTTPS/TLS connections.

You are advised to consult this Privacy Policy regularly for any changes, as continued use is deemed approval of all changes.
