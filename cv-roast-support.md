## Getting Started

CV Roast scans your résumé, finds the lines recruiters silently judge, roasts them, and shows you the fixed version.

### How to roast your first CV
1. Open CV Roast.
2. On the Home screen, tap **Roast My CV**.
3. Provide your CV one of three ways:
   - **Upload a document** — PDF, DOC, or DOCX
   - **Upload a photo** — a JPG, PNG, or HEIC image of your CV
   - **Paste text** — paste your résumé text directly
4. (Optional) Set your **target role** and pick a **tone** (Gen Z 🔥, Honest, or Professional).
5. Start the scan and watch the result come together.
6. On the result screen you'll see your **score**, your **CV archetype**, the number of issues found, and **one roasted worst line** for free. Unlock the full roast and every fix with a subscription.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Do I need to create an account?
No. CV Roast has no sign-up flow. An anonymous device account is created automatically the first time you open the app — no email, name, or password required.

### What does the free version include?
For free you get your **CV score**, your **CV archetype** badge, the **count of issues** detected, **one roasted worst line**, and **one anonymized share card**. The full roast across all weak lines, every before/after fix, role-specific feedback, ATS-style detail, all tone modes, and unlimited scans are unlocked with a subscription.

### What file types can I scan?
PDF, DOC, DOCX, and images (JPG, PNG, HEIC). For the most accurate analysis, a PDF or DOCX usually works best. HEIC images are converted on your device before upload.

### Is CV Roast a resume builder?
No. CV Roast does not build or store templates and is not an editor. It analyzes your existing CV, roasts the weak lines, and suggests rewritten versions you can copy into your own résumé.

### What is the "CV archetype"?
A fun, shareable badge summarizing your CV's vibe (for example, "Buzzword Warrior"). It's entertainment, not a personality test or a professional evaluation.

### What are the tone modes?
The same insight delivered in three voices: **Gen Z 🔥** (default), **Honest**, and **Professional**. You can set a default tone in Settings and switch tone on a result (premium).

### Is the ATS score official?
No. The ATS-style feedback is an indicator to help you think like an applicant-tracking system. It is not a certified score and is not affiliated with any employer or ATS vendor.

### Can I share my results?
Yes. You can export anonymized share cards (roast card, archetype card, before/after card). Personal information is **automatically hidden** before any card image is created — your CV is never shared, only the roast.

## Subscriptions & Billing

### What does the premium subscription unlock?
Premium unlocks the **full roast** of every weak line, **every before/after fix**, why-each-line-is-weak explanations, **role-specific feedback**, **ATS-style detail**, **all tone modes**, the full export/share pack, and **unlimited scans**.

### How do I subscribe?
Open the paywall (it appears at the result screen after your free teaser, or from the app) and choose a plan. Subscriptions are processed by **Apple via the App Store**.

### Is there a free trial?
No. Instead of a trial, the free teaser (your score, archetype, issue counts, and one roasted line) lets you judge the value before subscribing.

### How do I manage or cancel my subscription?
On iOS:
1. Open **Settings** on your device.
2. Tap your name at the top.
3. Tap **Subscriptions**.
4. Select **CV Roast** and tap **Cancel Subscription**.

You can also reach this from the in-app **Settings → Manage subscription** link.

### How do I get a refund?
Refunds are handled by Apple. To request one:
1. Visit https://reportaproblem.apple.com in a browser.
2. Sign in with your Apple ID.
3. Find your CV Roast purchase and follow the prompts.

The Service Provider cannot directly process App Store refunds.

### I subscribed but the app still shows the paywall.
1. Open CV Roast and go to **Settings → Restore purchases**.
2. Make sure you are signed into the same Apple ID that made the original purchase.
3. Ensure you have an internet connection.
4. If the issue persists, fully close and reopen the app.

If it still doesn't restore, email us at [support@nextline.software](mailto:support@nextline.software) with the purchase date and device model.

### Will my subscription sync across devices?
Subscriptions are tied to your **Apple ID**. Sign into the same Apple ID on another device, install CV Roast, and use **Restore purchases**.

## Troubleshooting

### The scan is stuck or taking too long.
- Make sure you have a stable internet connection (scanning requires a connection; browsing your history does not).
- If an error appears, tap **Retry**. A failed scan does not count against you.

### "Couldn't read your CV" / unreadable file
- For photo CVs, use a clear, well-lit, straight-on image; glare and blur reduce accuracy.
- Prefer a **PDF or DOCX** when possible for the most reliable analysis.
- Make sure the file actually contains résumé text (a near-empty or image-only document may be rejected).

### "Text too short" error
The Application needs enough text to analyze. Paste or upload a fuller version of your CV and try again.

### Photo picker shows "Permission Denied"
1. Open **Settings → CV Roast** on your device.
2. Tap **Photos**.
3. Choose **Selected Photos** or **All Photos** as preferred.
4. Return to CV Roast and try again.

### "Couldn't load plans" on the paywall
- Check your internet connection and tap **Retry**.
- Make sure the App Store is reachable and you're signed into an Apple ID.

### The app crashes when I open it
1. Force-close the app and reopen it.
2. Restart your device.
3. Update CV Roast to the latest version from the App Store.
4. If the crash persists, email us with your device model and iOS version.

## Privacy & Your CV

### Is my CV stored on your servers?
**No.** When you start a scan, your CV is sent securely to our backend, parsed **in memory** to extract its text, analyzed by the AI provider, and then discarded. We do **not** store your CV, the weak lines, or the generated roasts/fixes on our servers. Your roast history is kept **only on your device**. See the [Privacy Policy](cv-roast-privacy-policy.md) for full details.

### My CV contains my personal info — what happens to it?
Your CV is processed only to generate your analysis and is never stored by us. When you export a share card, personal information (name, email, phone, links) is **automatically redacted on your device** before the image is created. If a CV contains sensitive details you'd rather not transmit, remove those lines before scanning.

### Does CV Roast use my location?
No. CV Roast does not request, collect, or use location data.

### Does CV Roast track me across other apps?
No. CV Roast performs no cross-app tracking and does not show an App Tracking Transparency prompt. Product analytics are anonymous and never include the contents of your CV or results. The app shows no ads.

### Does CV Roast send notifications?
CV Roast does not use remote push notifications. It may show occasional **on-device reminder notifications**. You can turn these off in **iOS Settings → Notifications → CV Roast**.

### How do I delete my data?
You can:
1. Delete individual items from your **roast history** in the app.
2. **Uninstall the app** — this removes your local history and stops all collection from your device.
3. Optionally, email [support@nextline.software](mailto:support@nextline.software) to request deletion of your anonymous usage record. Because the account is anonymous, please mention your device model and approximate first-launch date so we can locate it.
