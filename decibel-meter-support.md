## Frequently Asked Questions

### General Questions

**Q: How do I get started with Decibel Meter?**
A: To begin using Decibel Meter, download the app from your device's app store, complete the setup process, and you're all set.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes. Decibel Meter is a local-first application — your measurement history, calibration settings, and preferences are stored on your device only and are not transmitted to our servers. Analytics and crash data sent to third-party services are transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS). For more information, refer to our [Privacy Policy](https://github.com/boraaltinok/privacy-policy/blob/main/decibel-meter-privacy-policy.md).

### Microphone & Permissions

**Q: Why does Decibel Meter need microphone access?**
A: The microphone is used to capture audio for real-time sound level measurement. All audio is processed entirely on your device — no audio recordings leave your phone.

**Q: The app shows 0 dB or no readings. What should I do?**
A: Make sure you have granted microphone permission in your device settings. Go to Settings > Decibel Meter > Microphone and ensure it is enabled.

**Q: How accurate are the measurements?**
A: Measurements are approximate and depend on your device's microphone hardware. The app is calibrated for general use but is not a substitute for professional sound level meters. You can fine-tune readings using the Calibration Offset in Settings > Advanced.

### Subscription & Premium

**Q: What do I get with Premium?**
A: Premium unlocks unlimited measurements, hearing safety insights, noise history tracking, noise report sharing, and an ad-free experience.

**Q: How do I restore my purchase on a new device?**
A: Go to Settings > Restore Purchases. Your subscription is linked to your App Store or Google Play account and will be restored automatically.

**Q: How do I cancel my subscription?**
A: Subscriptions are managed through your App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android) account. Go to your device's subscription settings to cancel.

### Technical Support

**Q: The app is not working properly. What should I do?**
A: Try these troubleshooting steps:

2. Restart your device
3. Ensure you have the latest app version
4. Confirm that microphone permission is granted

## Data Management

**Q: How do I delete my data?**
A: Your measurement data is stored locally on your device. You can delete it by clearing the app's data in your device settings, or by uninstalling the Application.
