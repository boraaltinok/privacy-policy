# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-06-24_

Thank you for using **GLP-1 Tracker** ("Application"). This Privacy Policy explains how NEXTLINE YAZILIM LİMİTED ŞİRKETİ ("Service Provider", "we", or "us") handles information when you use our Application.

The Application is a personal companion for people taking GLP-1 medications. It helps you log your weekly shot, track symptoms, appetite, and weight across each shot cycle, and prepare a provider-ready report. **All of your health information is stored only on your device and never leaves it.** We do not host user accounts, we do not have a server that stores your data, and the Application does not use any AI to process your health information.

## 1. Information We Handle

### Stored Locally on Your Device (not transmitted to us)

Everything you enter about your treatment stays on your device, in the Application's local database. We never receive, see, or store any of it. This includes:

- **Medications and prescriptions**: the medication you select, any custom label you enter, and the prescribed dose exactly as you typed it.
- **Schedule**: your shot weekday, time, cadence, and the timing anchor used to calculate your cycle.
- **Shots**: the date and time you log a shot, the injection site you select, the dose recorded at that time, and whether a shot was taken, skipped, or late.
- **Check-ins**: symptoms and their intensities (including any custom symptoms you add), appetite, food-noise, protein, water, and notes you choose to record for a day.
- **Weight entries**: weight values and dates you log, plus an optional start weight and goal weight.
- **Preferences**: units, appearance/theme, reminder settings, and what matters most to you.

This data lives only in the Application's on-device storage. Uninstalling the Application removes it. If iCloud Backup is enabled on your iOS device, your local data may be included in your iCloud Backup according to your Apple settings; this is a backup managed by Apple, not a sync to our servers.

### Information Automatically Collected by Third-Party SDKs

When you use the Application, certain technical information is collected by integrated third-party SDKs to operate, secure, and improve the Application. **None of your health information — your symptoms, weights, doses, injection sites, medication names, or medical-event dates — is ever sent to any of these SDKs or to us.**

- **Anonymous subscription identifier** generated for subscription tracking (RevenueCat). On Apple devices, RevenueCat may also collect an **Apple Search Ads attribution token** through Apple's AdServices framework to measure whether an App Store ad led to an install. This is a measurement signal handled by Apple and RevenueCat; it does **not** track you across other apps or websites.
- **Crash diagnostics** when the Application crashes, including device model, OS version, and the stack trace (Firebase Crashlytics).
- **Anonymous usage events** describing in-app actions in metadata form only — for example: which step of onboarding was reached, counts of certain actions, paywall trigger types, and the subscription plan identifier selected (Amplitude, Google Analytics for Firebase). These events carry only funnel flags, counts, and plan identifiers. The contents of your check-ins, symptom types or intensities, weights, doses, injection sites, and medical dates are **never** included.
- **Remote configuration**: on launch, the Application fetches feature toggles (such as which paywall variant to show) from Firebase Remote Config. This is an **inbound-only** read; we do not send any of your data with it.
- **Network-level data** such as IP address may be observed by these third-party SDKs as part of normal operation.

We do not collect:

- Your name, email, phone number, or any contact information.
- Your precise or approximate location.
- Photos, camera footage, contacts, calendar, or files outside the Application's own storage.
- The **IDFA / advertising identifier**. The Application does not use it.

The Application does **not** show third-party advertisements, does **not** include any ad SDK, and does **not** track you across other companies' apps or websites. Because of this, the Application does not present an App Tracking Transparency prompt.

### Notifications

The Application can send **local reminders** on your device for your shot day. These reminders are scheduled entirely on your device using Apple's local notification system. They are **not** push notifications and do not involve our servers or any remote service. The Application requests notification permission only when you set up reminders (after you choose a plan); you can decline, and you can change it any time in iOS Settings.

## 2. How We Use Information

We use the information described above only to:

- Provide the core tracking functionality (logging shots, check-ins, and weights, calculating your cycle, and preparing your report) — entirely on your device.
- Process subscription purchases and restore prior purchases via RevenueCat.
- Diagnose crashes and improve stability via Firebase Crashlytics.
- Understand aggregate, anonymous usage patterns to improve the Application via Amplitude and Google Analytics for Firebase.

We do not sell your information. We do not use your health data for advertising. We do not build advertising profiles. We do not use any AI service that processes your health data.

## 3. Third-Party Services

The Application integrates with the following third parties. Each has its own privacy policy that governs how it handles data on its side:

- [Firebase (Crashlytics, Analytics, Remote Config)](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy)
- [Amplitude](https://amplitude.com/privacy)
- [RevenueCat](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy)
- [Apple (App Store purchases, AdServices attribution)](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/)

We do **not** integrate with OpenAI, OpenRouter, or any third-party AI provider. The Application does not call any AI API.

## 4. Data Retention

**Local data**: stays on your device until you delete an entry, clear app storage, or uninstall the Application.

**Third-party data**: retention is governed by each provider's policy linked above. To request deletion of analytics data tied to your anonymous identifier, please contact us at [support@nextline.software](mailto:support@nextline.software).

## 5. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information we handle:

- All of your health information stays on your device — there is no server holding it to compromise.
- Subscription identifiers are anonymous (RevenueCat).
- Crash logs are scoped to crash data, not your health content.

No security system is perfect, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 6. Children's Privacy

The Application is intended for adults managing their own medication and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has used the Application, please contact us at [support@nextline.software](mailto:support@nextline.software) and we will take appropriate action.

## 7. Subscriptions

The Application offers optional Premium subscriptions managed by Apple's App Store and tracked via RevenueCat using an anonymous identifier. Apple handles your payment information; we do not receive your payment-card details. See Section 6 of the Terms & Conditions for subscription terms.

## 8. Exporting Your Data

You can export a copy of your on-device data at any time from **Settings → Export my data**. The Application builds a JSON snapshot of your data and hands it to the iOS share sheet so you can save or send it wherever you choose. This export is created on your device and is **not** uploaded to us or stored on any server.

## 9. Your Choices

- **Notifications**: local shot reminders can be enabled or disabled in the Application's Settings screen or in iOS Settings.
- **Analytics & Crash data**: you can stop all collection by uninstalling the Application.
- **Local data**: you can delete individual entries inside the Application, or remove all data by uninstalling.
- **Export**: you can export your data at any time from the Settings screen.

## 10. Not Medical Advice

The Application is a personal tracking and record-keeping tool. **It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, dosing guidance, or treatment recommendations.** It records what you enter — including your prescribed dose, which it stores exactly as you type it — and shows your own history back to you. It does not interpret your data as normal, abnormal, safe, or unsafe, and it does not tell you what to do. Always follow your prescriber's instructions and consult a qualified healthcare professional for any medical question.
