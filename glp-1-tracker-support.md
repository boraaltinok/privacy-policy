## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I log my shot?

On the Home tab, tap the log-shot action when your shot is due. You confirm the date and time, pick your injection site, and the dose on file is recorded with the entry. Logging a shot advances your cycle to your actual shot time, so a late shot moves your schedule forward rather than snapping back to a fixed weekday.

### What is a "cycle"?

A cycle is the period between your scheduled shots, based on the weekday, time, and cadence you set up. The Application uses this to show where you are in the current cycle and to organize your check-ins.

### How do I record symptoms, appetite, and weight?

Use the check-in flow to log symptoms (and their intensity), appetite, food-noise, protein, and water for a day — or mark that there is nothing to report. Weight is logged separately and appears on the Progress tab. You can also set an optional start weight and goal weight.

### Why is my dose shown exactly as I typed it?

By design. The Application stores and displays your prescribed dose verbatim and never parses, calculates, or adjusts it. This keeps your record faithful to what your prescriber gave you and avoids any implied medical interpretation.

### Where is my data stored?

All of your health information is stored locally on your device, in the Application's local database. It is **not** stored on our servers and never leaves your device. If iCloud Backup is enabled on your device, the local data may be included in your iCloud Backup according to your Apple settings.

### Can I sync my data across devices?

Cross-device sync is not available in the current version. Your data follows your device, with iCloud Backup as a passive restore mechanism if you migrate to a new device. You can also export a copy of your data (see below).

### How do I export my data?

Open **Settings → Export my data**. The Application creates a JSON snapshot of your data and opens the iOS share sheet so you can save or send it wherever you like. The export is created on your device and is not uploaded to us.

### How do shot reminders work?

Reminders are **local notifications** scheduled on your device for your shot day — there are no push notifications and no server involved. The Application asks for notification permission when you set up reminders (after choosing a plan). You can enable or disable reminders in the Application's Settings, or change the permission in **iOS Settings → GLP-1 Tracker → Notifications**.

### Why am I seeing a paywall?

Some features are part of a Premium subscription. The available plans, pricing, and any free trial are shown on the upgrade screen before you purchase, and on the App Store listing.

### How do I restore a previous purchase?

Open the restore option inside the Application. You must be signed in to the same Apple ID you used for the original purchase.

### How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed by Apple. Open **iOS Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions** to cancel or change your plan. Cancelling stops future renewals; you keep Premium access until the end of the current billing period.

### Will my data disappear if my subscription expires?

No. Your locally stored data stays on your device and remains readable. Only access to Premium features is limited until you renew.

### Does the app work offline?

Yes. Logging shots, check-ins, and weights, viewing your cycle and history, and preparing your report all work fully offline. An internet connection is only needed for subscription purchases and restore, crash and analytics reporting, and the launch-time remote configuration check.

### Does the app track me or show ads?

No. The Application shows no ads, includes no ad SDK, does not use the advertising identifier (IDFA), and does not track you across other apps or websites. It therefore does not show an App Tracking Transparency prompt. Anonymous, metadata-only usage and crash analytics are described in the Privacy Policy and never include your health information.

### How do I delete my data?

Delete individual entries inside the Application, or uninstall the Application to remove all locally stored data. To request deletion of analytics records tied to your anonymous identifier, email us at [support@nextline.software](mailto:support@nextline.software).

### I found a bug or have a feature request.

Please email [support@nextline.software](mailto:support@nextline.software) with a short description, your iOS version, and the Application version (visible in **Settings → About**). Screenshots are very helpful.
