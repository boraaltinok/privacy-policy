# Privacy Policy

**Application:** Sweep — Hidden Camera Detector
**Service Provider:** NEXTLINE YAZILIM LİMİTED ŞİRKETİ
**Last Updated:** June 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how NEXTLINE YAZILIM LİMİTED ŞİRKETİ ("we", "us", "our") handles information in connection with the **Sweep** mobile application (the "Application"). Privacy is the core purpose of this Application: nearly everything it does runs **entirely on your device**. The only feature that sends anything off your device is the optional **AI Room Inspection**, which is described in detail below.

The Application does **not** require you to create an account, and it does **not** ask for your name, email address, phone number, or any other directly identifying personal information.

---

## 1. Information We Process

### 1.1 Automatically Collected Data

When you use the Application, certain technical data is processed by our third-party SDKs to keep the app working, measure performance, and improve the product:

- Device and operating system information (device model, OS version, language, region)
- App usage and interaction events (which features and screens are used)
- Performance and diagnostic data, including crash reports and error logs
- Pseudonymous identifiers generated by our analytics, crash-reporting, and subscription providers
- Approximate (coarse) location, which may be inferred from your IP address by our analytics provider. We do **not** request the location permission and do **not** use GPS.

This data is associated with pseudonymous identifiers, **not** with your real-world identity. We do **not** track you across other companies' apps or websites.

### 1.2 Anonymous Authentication Data

The Application signs you in **anonymously** using Firebase Authentication on first launch. This creates a random, anonymous user identifier so the Application can enforce fair-use limits on the AI Room Inspection feature. **No account, login, email, or password is involved**, and this identifier is not linked to your personal identity.

### 1.3 AI Room Inspection Data (the only data that leaves your device)

The **AI Room Inspection** feature is the single feature that transmits data off your device, and only when you choose to use it. When you take a photo for AI Room Inspection:

- The photo is **downscaled** on your device and its **EXIF/location (GPS) metadata is stripped** before anything is sent.
- The processed image, together with your selected language and a one-time request key, is transmitted securely over HTTPS to our backend (a Firebase Cloud Function), which forwards it to **OpenRouter** for AI vision analysis.
- The AI returns a list of objects and areas in the room that may be worth physically inspecting, with reasons and suggested physical checks.
- The photo is processed **in-request and is never stored** on our servers or by us.

To operate this feature fairly, our backend keeps a small **usage counter** associated with your anonymous identifier (the number of inspections used today and totals). This counter exists only to enforce daily fair-use limits and to allow safe retries; it does **not** contain your photos or personal information.

### 1.4 Local Network Scan Data

The Wi-Fi Device Scan inspects your local network to list connected devices and flag ones worth a closer look. This scan runs **entirely on your device**. Device information discovered on your network (such as IP addresses, hostnames, open ports, and Bonjour/mDNS services) is processed locally to classify devices and is **not** transmitted to us.

### 1.5 Sensor Data

The magnetic-field scan reads your device's **magnetometer** sensor, and the lens/infrared inspection uses your device **camera** viewfinder. This sensor and camera data is processed **on your device in real time** for the scan and is **not** uploaded or stored on our servers. (Photos used for AI Room Inspection are the sole exception and are covered in Section 1.3.)

### 1.6 Local Device Data

The following data is created and stored **only on your device**:

- Saved scan reports and scan history
- App preferences and settings
- Onboarding and funnel flags
- Cached summary of your most recent scan

You can delete this data at any time by clearing the app's data or by uninstalling the Application.

## 3. Permissions

The Application requests only the permissions it needs to function:

| Permission | Why it is used |
|------------|----------------|
| **Camera** | Lens/infrared reflection inspection (on-device) and capturing a photo for the optional AI Room Inspection. |
| **Local Network** (Bonjour/mDNS) | Scanning your Wi-Fi network to list devices and flag possible cameras. Runs on your device. |
| **Notifications** | Sending local reminders (for example, travel-safety reminders). These are generated on your device. |

The Application does **not** use the App Tracking Transparency prompt and does **not** track you across other companies' apps or websites.

The magnetometer sensor does not require a system permission prompt.
