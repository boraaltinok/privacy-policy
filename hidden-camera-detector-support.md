## Getting Started

Sweep is a travel-first hidden-camera detector for hotels, Airbnbs, and rentals. It guides you through a 5-check sweep of a room and produces an honest scan report. It is available for iOS and Android.

### Key Features

- **Wi-Fi Device Scan** — lists devices on the local network and flags ones worth a closer look (on-device).
- **AI Room Inspection** — analyzes a room photo to suggest objects and areas worth physically checking.
- **Lens / Infrared Inspection** — uses the camera to help you spot lens reflections.
- **Magnetic-Field Scan** — uses the magnetometer to detect magnetic anomalies.
- **Guided Physical Sweep & Scan Report** — a step-by-step checklist and a saved report.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General

**Q: Can Sweep guarantee a room has no hidden cameras?**
No. Sweep is an assistive aid. It highlights signals and areas worth inspecting, but it cannot confirm the presence or absence of a camera, and a clean scan does not mean a room is "safe." Always inspect physically.

**Q: Do my scans leave my device?**
The Wi-Fi scan, magnetic-field scan, and lens/infrared inspection run entirely on your device. The only feature that sends data off your device is the optional AI Room Inspection, which sends a downscaled, location-stripped photo for analysis. That photo is processed for the scan only and is not stored.

### Permissions

**Q: Why does Sweep need the Camera permission?**
The camera is used for the lens/infrared reflection inspection (on-device) and to capture a photo for AI Room Inspection.

**Q: Why does Sweep need Local Network access?**
To scan your Wi-Fi network and list connected devices so it can flag possible cameras. This scan stays on your device.

**Q: Does Sweep track me across other apps or websites?**
No. Sweep does not track you across other companies' apps or websites and does not show the App Tracking Transparency prompt. We use privacy-respecting analytics and crash reporting only to keep the app working and improve it.

### AI Room Inspection

**Q: AI Room Inspection shows an error or won't run. What's wrong?**
AI Room Inspection requires an internet connection. If the service is temporarily unavailable, you'll see a retryable "Inspection failed — Retry" message. There is also a daily fair-use limit; if you reach it, try again the next day or upgrade for a higher limit.

**Q: Are my AI Room Inspection photos saved?**
No. Photos are downscaled and stripped of location metadata on your device, processed in-request, and never stored on our servers.

### Scans & Hardware

**Q: A scan shows "unavailable" or empty results.**
Camera, magnetometer, and Wi-Fi scanning require a real device with the relevant hardware and an active Wi-Fi connection. On simulators or devices lacking a sensor, the relevant scan will show an unavailable state.

### Subscription & Premium

**Q: What's free and what's premium?**
The manual tools (magnetic-field scan, lens/infrared inspection, basic physical checklist, and a basic Wi-Fi scan with a flagged count) are always free. Premium unlocks full device details, the "why flagged" evidence, AI Room Inspection, the complete guided sweep, saved scan reports, scan history, and unlimited scans.

**Q: How do I start the free trial and what does it cost?**

**Q: How do I restore my purchase?**

**Q: How do I cancel?**

## Data Management

**Q: How do I delete my data?**
All scan reports, history, and preferences are stored only on your device. You can delete them by clearing the app's data or by uninstalling the Application.

**Q: How do I share a scan report?**
You can share a report's summary card using your device's standard share sheet from within the Application.
