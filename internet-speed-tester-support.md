## 2. Speed Test Guide

### 2.1 How to Run a Speed Test
1. Open the app and tap the **Start Test** button
2. The app will automatically measure:
   - **Download Speed** (Mbps) - How fast data comes to your device
   - **Upload Speed** (Mbps) - How fast data goes from your device
   - **Ping/Latency** (ms) - How quickly your device communicates with servers
3. Wait for all three measurements to complete
4. View your results with a quality rating and plain-language explanation

### 2.2 Understanding Your Results

**Quality Ratings:**

| Rating | Download Speed | What It Means |
|--------|---------------|---------------|
| Excellent | 50+ Mbps | Great for 4K streaming, large downloads, video calls |
| Good | 25-50 Mbps | Handles most activities smoothly |
| Fair | 10-25 Mbps | Adequate for basic browsing and standard streaming |
| Poor | 5-10 Mbps | May struggle with video streaming and large downloads |
| Bad | Below 5 Mbps | Limited functionality, slow loading times |

**Ping/Latency:**
- Under 20ms: Excellent (great for gaming and video calls)
- 20-50ms: Good (smooth for most activities)
- 50-100ms: Fair (noticeable delay in real-time activities)
- Over 100ms: Poor (significant delays)

### 2.3 Best Practices for Accurate Results

**For Optimal Measurements:**
- Close other apps that may be using your internet connection
- Move closer to your Wi-Fi router if testing Wi-Fi
- Run multiple tests at different times of day for a complete picture
- Ensure no large downloads or uploads are running in the background
- Test on both Wi-Fi and cellular to compare connection types

### 2.4 Connection Types
The app automatically detects your connection type:
- **Wi-Fi**: Connected to a wireless network
- **Cellular**: Using mobile data (4G/5G)
- The connection type is shown with each test result

## 6. Privacy and Data Information

### 6.1 What Data We Collect
**Speed Test Data:**
- Test results are stored locally on your device only
- No speed test data is uploaded to our servers
- Your IP address is exposed to speed test servers during testing (this is inherent to network measurement)

**Analytics:**
- Anonymous usage patterns to improve the app experience
- Crash reports to fix technical issues
- No personally identifiable information is collected

### 6.2 Data Security
**Local Storage:**
- All app data stays on your device
- No account creation required (no sign-in needed)
- No personal information collected (no name, email, or phone number)

**Network Security:**
- Analytics and crash data use secure HTTPS connections
- Anonymous user identification only

### 6.3 Your Choices
- Disable notifications in device settings
- Manage ad tracking preferences in iOS Settings > Privacy > Tracking
- Purchase ad removal to eliminate all ad-related data collection
- Uninstall the app to stop all data collection and remove local data

## 7. Common Issues and Solutions

### 7.1 Speed Test Issues

**Test Not Starting:**
1. Check your internet connection (Wi-Fi or cellular)
2. Try toggling airplane mode on and off
3. Restart the app
4. Restart your device if the issue persists

**Results Seem Inaccurate:**
1. Close other apps using internet bandwidth
2. Move closer to your Wi-Fi router
3. Run multiple tests at different times
4. Test on a different network for comparison
5. Remember that results naturally vary based on network conditions

**Test Takes Too Long:**
1. You may have a very slow connection -- the test needs time to measure accurately
2. Check if your connection is working at all by opening a website
3. Try again later when network conditions may be better

### 7.2 Purchase Issues

**Ad Removal Not Activating:**
1. Verify payment was processed in App Store/Google Play
2. Restart the app to refresh purchase status
3. Check your internet connection
4. Try the "Restore Purchases" option in Settings
5. Contact support if the issue persists

### 7.3 Notification Issues

**Receiving Unwanted Notifications:**
1. Disable notifications in your device settings
2. Purchasing ad removal also disables reminder notifications

**Not Receiving Notifications:**
1. Check notification permissions in your device settings
2. Ensure notifications are not silenced or in Focus mode (iOS)

### 7.4 Performance Issues

**App Running Slowly:**
1. Close other apps running in the background
2. Restart your device
3. Ensure adequate storage space is available
4. Update to the latest app version

**Crashes or Freezing:**
1. Update to the latest version from the App Store
2. Restart your device
3. Reinstall the app if problems persist (note: test history will be lost)
