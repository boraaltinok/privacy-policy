## 2. Ticket Scanning Guide

### 2.1 How to Scan a Ticket
1. Tap the **Scan** tab at the bottom of the screen
2. Point your camera at your Mega Millions ticket
3. Align the ticket within the gold scanning rectangle
4. The app will automatically capture and process the image
5. Review the extracted numbers on the confirmation screen
6. Edit any numbers if needed, then tap **Check Numbers**
7. View your results — matched numbers will be highlighted

### 2.2 Best Scanning Practices
**For Optimal Results:**
- Ensure good lighting (natural light preferred)
- Hold the camera steady and focus on the ticket
- Keep the ticket flat and fully visible in the frame
- Avoid glare, shadows, or reflections on the ticket
- Make sure all numbers are clearly visible

**Ticket Positioning:**
- Center the ticket in the camera view
- Ensure all printed numbers and dates are visible
- Maintain a consistent distance (6-12 inches from ticket)
- Keep the ticket perpendicular to the camera

### 2.3 Gallery Upload
If you prefer not to use the camera:
1. Tap the gallery icon on the scan screen
2. Select a photo of your ticket from your photo library
3. The app will process the image the same as a camera capture

### 2.4 Troubleshooting Scans
**If Numbers Are Not Extracted Correctly:**
1. Try scanning in better lighting conditions
2. Clean your camera lens if the image is blurry
3. Make sure the ticket is not wrinkled or folded
4. Try a different angle if there is glare on the ticket
5. Use the edit feature on the confirmation screen to correct any numbers
6. If scanning fails entirely, use the **Create Ticket** feature to enter numbers manually

**Scan Errors:**
- "Couldn't read ticket" — Try again with better lighting or a different angle
- "No numbers found" — Ensure you're scanning a valid Mega Millions ticket
- "Connection error" — Check your internet connection (scanning requires internet)

## 3. Creating Tickets Manually

### 3.1 Enter Numbers
1. Go to the **Create** tab
2. Select **Enter Numbers**
3. Choose 5 white ball numbers (1-70) and 1 Mega Ball number (1-25)
4. Toggle Megaplier on/off
5. Select a draw date
6. Tap **Check Numbers** to see results

### 3.2 Generate Random Numbers
1. Go to the **Create** tab
2. Select **Generate Random**
3. The app will generate a random set of Mega Millions numbers
4. You can regenerate for new random numbers
5. Tap **Check Numbers** to verify against a specific drawing

## 7. Common Issues and Solutions

### 7.1 Camera and Scanning Issues
**Camera Won't Open:**
1. Check camera permissions in your device settings
2. Restart the app
3. Ensure no other apps are using the camera
4. Restart your device if the issue persists

**Poor Scan Accuracy:**
1. Improve lighting conditions
2. Clean your camera lens
3. Hold the phone steady
4. Use the manual edit feature to correct extracted numbers

### 7.2 Subscription and Payment Issues
**Subscription Not Activating:**
1. Verify payment was processed in App Store/Google Play
2. Restart the app to refresh subscription status
3. Check your internet connection
4. Try the "Restore Purchases" option in Settings
5. Contact support if the issue persists beyond 24 hours

**Can't Cancel Subscription:**
- Cancellation must be done through the App Store or Google Play, not within the app
- Follow the platform-specific instructions in Section 5.4 above

### 7.3 Results and Data Issues
**Drawing Results Not Loading:**
1. Check your internet connection
2. Pull down to refresh on the home screen
3. Restart the app
4. Results may take a short time to update after a drawing

**Incorrect Results:**
1. Verify you scanned the correct ticket
2. Check the draw date matches your ticket
3. Use the edit feature to correct any misread numbers
4. Always verify with official lottery sources

### 7.4 Performance Issues
**App Running Slowly:**
1. Close other apps running in the background
2. Restart your device
3. Ensure adequate storage space is available
4. Update to the latest app version

**Crashes or Freezing:**
1. Update to the latest version from the App Store
2. Restart your device
3. Reinstall the app if problems persist (note: local data will be lost)
