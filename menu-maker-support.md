# Menu Maker - Support

Welcome to the Menu Maker support page. We're here to help you build, publish, and manage your menus with ease.

## Getting Started

Menu Maker helps small food businesses build a menu once and turn it into a print-ready PDF or image and a live, hosted QR web menu. The app is compatible with iOS devices.

### Key Features
- **Menu Builder**: Create and edit menus with dishes, descriptions, prices, and venue details
- **AI Import**: Scan a paper menu with your camera and let AI turn it into a structured menu
- **QR Web Menu**: Publish your menu to a public web address with its own QR code
- **Export & Print**: Export your menu as a PDF or image and share it
- **Translation** (Premium): Translate your menu into other languages

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Building Your Menu

**Q: How do I create a menu?**
A: Open the app and start a new menu. You can add sections, dishes, descriptions, prices, and your venue details manually, or use AI Import to get a head start from a photo of an existing menu.

**Q: Can I add my venue logo?**
A: Yes. You can select a logo or images from your photo library. A logo or image you add can be included in your published web menu.

### Scanning & AI Import

**Q: How does AI Import work?**
A: With your permission, the app uses your camera to scan a paper menu (or you can pick a photo from your library). The photo is securely uploaded to our backend, where an AI service extracts the text into a structured menu you can edit. The free tier includes 1 AI import; Premium unlocks unlimited imports.

**Q: Are the AI results always accurate?**
A: Not always. AI extraction and translation are automated and may contain errors or omissions. Always review and correct the imported dishes, descriptions, and prices before publishing or printing your menu.

**Q: Why does Menu Maker need camera and photo access?**
A: The camera is used to scan paper menus for AI Import, and photo-library access lets you pick menu photos and your venue logo. You can grant or deny these permissions in your device settings.

### Publishing Your QR Web Menu

**Q: How do I publish my menu?**
A: Once your menu is ready, use the publish option to host it online. Your menu becomes available at a public web address with its own QR code that customers can scan. Publishing is a Premium feature.

**Q: Who can see my published menu?**
A: A published menu is **public by design** — anyone who has the QR code or link can view it. Only publish information you are comfortable making publicly available.

**Q: How do I update or unpublish my menu?**
A: Edit your menu in the app and publish again to update what customers see. You can also unpublish a menu to remove it from its public web address.

### Exporting & Printing

**Q: How do I export or print my menu?**
A: Use the export option to generate a print-ready PDF or image, then share it via your device's share sheet or send it to a printer. Export is a Premium feature.

### Translation

**Q: How do I translate my menu?**
A: Premium users can translate menu text into other languages. Translation is performed by an AI service, so please review the results for accuracy before publishing.

### Subscription & Premium

**Q: What do I get with Premium?**
A: Premium unlocks exporting (PDF/image), publishing your QR web menu, menu translation, and unlimited AI imports. The free tier lets you build and preview a menu and includes 1 AI import.

**Q: How do I restore my purchase on a new device?**
A: Go to Settings and choose Restore Purchases. Your subscription is linked to your App Store account and will be restored automatically.

**Q: How do I cancel my subscription?**
A: Subscriptions are auto-renewable and managed through your App Store account. Go to your device's subscription settings to turn off auto-renewal or cancel. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period.

### Offline Use & Data

**Q: Can I use the app offline?**
A: Yes — you can build, edit, and preview menus offline. Features such as AI Import, translation, and publishing require an internet connection.

**Q: Where is my menu data stored?**
A: The menus you build are stored locally on your device. Data leaves your device only when you use AI Import or translation, or when you choose to publish a menu. See our [Privacy Policy](https://github.com/boraaltinok/privacy-policy/blob/main/menu-maker-privacy-policy.md) for details.

**Q: How do I delete my data?**
A: Your menus are stored locally on your device. You can delete them by clearing the app's data in your device settings or by uninstalling the app. If you have published a menu and want it removed from its public web address, unpublish it in the app or contact support.

### Technical Support

**Q: The app is not working properly. What should I do?**
A: Try these troubleshooting steps:
1. Close and reopen the app
2. Restart your device
3. Ensure you have the latest app version
4. Confirm that camera and photo permissions are granted for AI Import
5. Check your internet connection for AI Import, translation, and publishing

**Q: How do I update the app?**
A: App updates are typically automatic. You can also manually check for updates in your device's app store.
