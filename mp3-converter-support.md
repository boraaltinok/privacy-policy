## Getting Started

MP3 Converter is an on-device, offline audio extractor. Import a video or media file you already have — from the Files app, your photo library, or a direct media link — choose your format and bitrate, and convert it into a real, full-length audio file (MP3, M4A, WAV, AAC, or FLAC) or a short ringtone. Everything is processed locally on your device, so your media never leaves your phone unless you choose to share it. Designed for anyone who needs quick, reliable audio extraction without uploads.

### Key Features
- **Audio Extraction**: Pull the audio track out of a video and save it as an audio file.
- **Multiple Formats**: Convert to MP3, M4A, WAV, AAC, or FLAC, or create a short (≤30s) ringtone.
- **Bitrate Control**: Choose the bitrate to balance quality and file size.
- **Flexible Import**: Import from the Files app / iCloud Drive, from your photo library, or from a direct media link.
- **Integrity Gate**: Every conversion is verified, so you never end up with an empty or broken file.
- **Inline Player**: Preview your converted audio before you save or share it.
- **Library**: Access your previously converted files any time from the Library tab.
- **Easy Sharing**: Export and share your audio via the native share sheet or save it to Files.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General Questions

**Q: How do I get started with MP3 Converter?**
A: Download the app from the App Store, complete the brief onboarding, and you're ready to convert. Tap to add a source (a file, a video from your photos, or a media link), pick your format and bitrate, and convert.

**Q: How does the conversion work?**
A: When you select a file or paste a link, the app extracts and converts the audio entirely on your device using an embedded, offline processing engine. No media is uploaded to any server.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes. All audio extraction and conversion happens locally on your device. Your source media and converted files are never uploaded to our servers or any external cloud service. For more details, please review our [Privacy Policy](https://github.com/boraaltinok/privacy-policy/blob/main/mp3-converter-privacy-policy.md).

**Q: Does MP3 Converter work offline?**
A: Yes. Converting a file that is already on your device works without an internet connection. An internet connection is only needed for optional features such as importing from a media link, validating your subscription, and showing ads.

**Q: What audio formats can I export?**
A: MP3, M4A, WAV, AAC, and FLAC, plus a short ringtone format (≤30 seconds).

### Conversion Questions

**Q: Which files can I import?**
A: You can import video or audio files from the Files app / iCloud Drive, pick a video from your photo library, or paste a direct media link that the app can download.

**Q: How do I import from a link?**
A: Choose the media-link option when adding a source and paste a direct link to the media file. The app downloads it from that source to your device and converts it locally. You are responsible for ensuring you have the right to use the linked content.

**Q: Why did my conversion fail or produce a warning?**
A: The app verifies every output and refuses to save an empty or invalid file. A failure usually means the source file is unsupported, corrupted, protected, or a link could not be downloaded. Try a different source file, check the link, and make sure you have enough free storage.

**Q: How do I change the format or bitrate?**
A: Pick your format and bitrate on the conversion setup screen before converting. You can also set defaults in the app.

**Q: Can I make a ringtone?**
A: Yes. Choose the ringtone option to create a short clip (up to 30 seconds).

### Subscription and Billing

**Q: What's included in the free version?**
A: The free version includes core conversion. Some formats, bitrates, and features are limited, and free exports may require watching a short optional rewarded ad.

**Q: What does the premium subscription include?**
A: Premium subscribers get all formats and bitrates, an ad-free experience, and other premium features.

**Q: How do I manage or cancel my subscription?**
A: You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time through your iPhone's Settings:
1. Open **Settings** on your iPhone
2. Tap your **Apple ID** at the top
3. Tap **Subscriptions**
4. Find **MP3 Converter** and manage from there

**Q: How do I restore a purchase?**
A: If you reinstalled the app or switched devices, use the "Restore Purchases" option on the paywall or settings screen to restore your active subscription.

**Q: How do I request a refund?**
A: Refunds are handled by Apple. You can request a refund through Apple's [Report a Problem](https://reportaproblem.apple.com/) page or by contacting Apple Support.

**Q: Can I watch an ad instead of subscribing?**
A: In some cases, yes. Free users may watch a short rewarded ad to unlock an export instead of subscribing.

### Technical Support

**Q: The app is not working properly. What should I do?**
A: If you encounter issues, try these troubleshooting steps:
1. Close and restart the app.
2. Restart your device.
3. Ensure you have the latest app version from the App Store.
4. Check that you've granted the necessary permissions.

**Q: I can't select a video from my photo library.**
A: Make sure you've granted photo library permission:
1. Open **Settings** on your iPhone
2. Scroll down and tap **MP3 Converter**
3. Enable **Photos** access (select "All Photos" for full access)

**Q: Where are my converted files saved?**
A: Converted files are stored within the app and listed in the **Library** tab. To save a file to your Files app or share it, use the export/share button.

**Q: How do I update the app?**
A: App updates are typically automatic. You can also manually check for updates in the App Store by searching for MP3 Converter and tapping "Update" if available.

## Data Management

**Q: How do I delete my data?**
A: All your data (converted files, preferences, library history) is stored locally on your device. Deleting items from the Library removes them, and uninstalling the app will permanently delete all app data. For deletion of any analytics data collected by our third-party services, please contact us at support@nextline.software.

**Q: Can I recover deleted files?**
A: Once a file is deleted from the app or the app is uninstalled, it cannot be recovered. We recommend exporting important files to your Files app before deleting.
