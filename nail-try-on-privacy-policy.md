## 1. Information Collection and Use

### 1.1 Automatically Collected Information
When you download and use the Application, certain data may be automatically collected, such as:
- **IP Address** (Internet Protocol address)
- **Device type, model, and operating system version**
- **An anonymous app identifier** assigned to your installation (see Section 2)
- **Screens visited within the Application, time spent, and dates/times of use**
- **Diagnostic and crash data**

We use this automatically collected information for analytics, troubleshooting, measuring feature performance, and improving the Application.

### 1.2 Photos You Provide
To create a nail preview, the Application processes photos you choose to provide:
- A **photo of your hand** (selected from your photo library or taken with your camera).
- Optionally, an **inspiration photo** of a nail look you want to try.

These photos are uploaded over a secure (TLS/HTTPS) connection to our cloud infrastructure (Google Firebase Storage) and sent to our AI processing provider solely to generate your nail preview. See Section 3 for how images are processed and Section 4 for retention.

The Application accesses your camera and photo library **only** when you choose to add a photo or save a result. It does not browse, scan, or collect any other photos from your device.

### 1.3 Location Data
The Application does **not** collect or use the location of your mobile device.

### 1.4 Data Stored Only on Your Device
Nail looks you choose to **save** are stored **locally on your device** so they remain available offline and after our server-side copies expire. This saved-looks library is not uploaded to or stored on our servers, and is removed when you delete a saved look or uninstall the Application.

## 2. Identity and Accounts

The Application uses **Firebase Anonymous Authentication** to assign a randomly generated, anonymous identifier to your installation. This identifier lets the Application securely associate your uploads and results with your device and enforce fair-use limits.

- We do **not** operate user accounts, logins, profiles, or passwords.
- We do **not** collect your name, email address, phone number, or social media identity.
- The anonymous identifier is **not** linked to your real-world identity.

---

## 3. AI Image Processing

To generate your nail preview, the photo of your hand (and your inspiration photo or selected color/finish/vibe) is transmitted securely over TLS to our AI image-processing provider, **OpenRouter**, which routes the request to the underlying image-generation model provider (currently Google's Gemini image model). The model edits the nails in your photo to produce a single preview, which is returned to the Application.

- Your photos are used **only** to create the preview you requested.
- We do **not** sell your photos, and we do **not** use your photos to build advertising profiles.
- We do **not** use your photos to train our own AI models, and we instruct our processing providers to process them solely to deliver your preview.
- Processing providers may temporarily retain inputs and outputs for service delivery and abuse monitoring under their own policies, after which the data is deleted.

By using the Application, you consent to your photos being processed under these conditions. Relevant third-party policies are linked in Section 5.

---

## 4. Data Retention

- **Uploaded photos and generated results** stored on our cloud infrastructure are **automatically deleted within 7 days**.
- **Saved looks** that you explicitly save are kept **only on your device** until you delete them or uninstall the Application.
- **Analytics, diagnostic, and subscription records** are retained by the third-party providers in Section 5 according to their respective policies.

If you would like us to delete data associated with your installation, please contact us at [support@nextline.software](mailto:support@nextline.software) and we will respond within a reasonable timeframe.
