# Nail Try-On: Salon Preview — Support

Welcome to the Nail Try-On support page. We're here to help you preview nail looks on your own hand with confidence before your salon visit.

## Getting Started

Nail Try-On lets you see a nail look on a photo of **your own hand** before you commit to it at the salon. There's no account to create — just open the app and start.

1. From the home screen, choose how you want to start:
   - **Use inspo** — upload a saved nail photo you want to try.
   - **Try colors** — pick a color family and finish (nude, red, French, chrome, jelly, milky pink, and more).
   - **Find ideas** — pick a vibe or occasion and let the app suggest a look.
2. Upload or take a clear photo of your hand.
3. Wait a few seconds while your preview is created.
4. View your nail look, save it for the salon, or share a before/after.

This app is available on iOS.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General

**Q: Do I need an account to use Nail Try-On?**
A: No. There is no sign-up or login. The app works anonymously — you don't provide your name or email.

**Q: How does the preview work?**
A: You upload a photo of your hand, and the app uses secure AI image processing to show a nail look applied to your hand. Previews are realistic visualizations, not a guarantee of an exact salon result — your nail technician, products, and nail length all affect the final look.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes. Photos are uploaded over an encrypted (HTTPS/TLS) connection, stored in access-scoped, non-public locations, and automatically deleted from our servers within 7 days. For details, see our [Privacy Policy](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/boraaltinok/privacy-policy/main/nail-try-on-privacy-policy.md).

### Photos and Permissions

**Q: Why does the app ask for camera and photo access?**
A: Camera access lets you take a photo of your hand or inspiration; photo library access lets you choose an existing photo and save your finished looks. The app only accesses photos you select — it does not scan your library.

**Q: I denied camera or photo permission. How do I enable it?**
A: Open your device's **Settings → Nail Try-On** and turn on Camera and/or Photos. Then return to the app and try again.

**Q: What makes a good hand photo?**
A: Use good lighting, keep your hand and nails clearly visible and in focus, and avoid heavy shadows or busy backgrounds for the best preview.

### Results and Saving

**Q: Where are my saved looks stored?**
A: Saved looks are stored **on your device** so they stay available offline. Uploaded photos and freshly generated results on our servers are deleted within 7 days, so save any look you want to keep.

**Q: Can I save a look to my photo library or share it?**
A: Yes. You can save a finished look to your Photos and share a before/after or a salon-ready card to show your nail technician.

### Subscriptions

**Q: How does billing work?**
A: Premium features are offered through an auto-renewing subscription with a free trial, billed through your app store account. Manage or cancel anytime in your app store subscription settings.

**Q: How do I restore my subscription?**
A: On the paywall or settings screen, tap **Restore Purchases** while signed in to the same app store account you used to subscribe.

### Troubleshooting

**Q: A preview failed to generate. What should I do?**
A: Try these steps:
1. Check your internet connection.
2. Make sure your hand and nails are clearly visible in the photo.
3. Close and reopen the app, then try again.
4. Ensure you're using the latest app version.

## Data and Privacy Requests

**Q: How do I request deletion of my data?**
A: Uploaded photos and results are automatically deleted within 7 days, and saved looks live only on your device (uninstalling removes them). If you'd like us to delete data associated with your installation, contact us at [support@nextline.software](mailto:support@nextline.software).
