## Getting Started

Image to PDF Converter is a powerful mobile application that lets you convert photos into professional PDF documents. Whether you need to digitize receipts, create multi-page documents from photos, or scan documents on the go, our app handles it all with fast, on-device processing. Designed for students, professionals, and freelancers who need quick and reliable PDF creation.

### Key Features
- **Image to PDF Conversion**: Select multiple photos from your gallery or capture new ones with your camera, then combine them into a single PDF document.
- **Document Scanner**: Use Apple's advanced VisionKit technology to scan documents with automatic edge detection and perspective correction.
- **Customizable Output**: Choose page size (A4, Letter, Legal, Fit), orientation (Portrait, Landscape, Auto), and quality settings (Standard, High).
- **PDF Preview**: Preview your PDF before exporting to ensure everything looks perfect.
- **Easy Sharing**: Export and share your PDFs via the native share sheet, save to Files, or print directly from the app.
- **Recent Files**: Access your recently created PDFs for quick sharing or reference.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General Questions

**Q: How do I get started with Image to PDF Converter?**
A: Simply download the app from the App Store, complete the brief onboarding, and you're ready to create PDFs. Tap "Image to PDF" to select photos, or "Scan to PDF" to scan documents with your camera.

**Q: How does the PDF conversion work?**
A: When you select or capture images, the app processes them entirely on your device. Images are compressed and arranged according to your settings, then combined into a PDF file. No images are uploaded to any server.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes. All image processing and PDF generation happens locally on your device. Your photos and PDFs are never uploaded to our servers or any external cloud service. For more details, please review our [Privacy Policy](https://github.com/boraaltinok/privacy-policy/blob/main/pdf-converter-privacy-policy.md).

**Q: How many images can I add to a single PDF?**
A: You can add up to 30 images per PDF document.

**Q: What image formats are supported?**
A: The app supports all common image formats including JPEG, PNG, and HEIC (iPhone default format).

### PDF Creation Questions

**Q: How do I change the page size or orientation?**
A: Go to Settings within the app to change your default page size (A4, Letter, Legal, or Fit) and orientation (Portrait, Landscape, or Auto). These settings apply to all new PDFs you create.

**Q: What's the difference between quality settings?**
A:
- **Standard**: Smaller file size, suitable for most documents
- **High**: Best quality output, good balance between quality and file size

**Q: Can I rearrange pages before creating the PDF?**
A: Yes. After selecting your images, you can drag and drop to reorder them, rotate individual pages, or remove unwanted images before generating the PDF.

**Q: Why does my PDF look blurry?**
A: This may happen if the source images are low resolution. Try using the High quality setting in the app's settings. For document scanning, ensure good lighting and hold your device steady.

### Document Scanner Questions

**Q: How does the document scanner work?**
A: The scanner uses Apple's native VisionKit technology to detect document edges, correct perspective, and enhance readability. Simply point your camera at a document and the scanner will automatically detect and capture it.

**Q: Can I scan multiple pages in one session?**
A: Yes. The scanner allows you to capture multiple pages in sequence, which are then combined into a single PDF.

### Technical Support

**Q: The app is not working properly. What should I do?**
A: If you encounter issues, try these troubleshooting steps:
1. Close and restart the app.
2. Restart your device.
3. Ensure you have the latest app version from the App Store.
4. Check that you've granted the necessary permissions (camera, photo library).

**Q: The camera isn't working in the app.**
A: Make sure you've granted camera permission to Image to PDF Converter:
1. Open **Settings** on your iPhone
2. Scroll down and tap **Image to PDF Converter**
3. Enable **Camera** access

**Q: I can't select images from my photo library.**
A: Make sure you've granted photo library permission:
1. Open **Settings** on your iPhone
2. Scroll down and tap **Image to PDF Converter**
3. Enable **Photos** access (select "All Photos" for full access)

**Q: Where are my PDFs saved?**
A: Generated PDFs are stored within the app. You can access them from the "Recents" section on the home screen. To save a PDF to your Files app or share it, use the export/share button.

**Q: How do I update the app?**
A: App updates are typically automatic. You can also manually check for updates in the App Store by searching for Image to PDF Converter and tapping "Update" if available.

## Account and Data Management

**Q: How do I delete my data?**
A: All your data (PDFs, preferences, history) is stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all app data. For deletion of any analytics data collected by our third-party services, please contact us at support@nextline.software.

**Q: Can I recover deleted PDFs?**
A: Once a PDF is deleted from the app or the app is uninstalled, the files cannot be recovered. We recommend exporting important PDFs to your Files app or cloud storage before deleting.
