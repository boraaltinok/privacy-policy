## 2. Photo Library Data

The Application accesses your device's photo library to scan for duplicates, similar photos, screenshots, and large videos. **All photo analysis is performed entirely on your device.** The Application does not upload, transmit, or store your photos on any server.

Only derived metadata — such as photo counts, file sizes, category groupings, and compact numerical fingerprints used for similarity detection — is stored locally on your device. These fingerprints are mathematical representations and cannot be used to reconstruct your original photos.

Photos are never:
- **Uploaded** to our servers or any third-party service
- **Transmitted** over the internet
- **Accessed** by the Service Provider or any third party

When you choose to delete photos, the Application uses your device's native deletion flow, which moves items to the "Recently Deleted" album. You can recover deleted photos within Apple's standard 30-day recovery window.
