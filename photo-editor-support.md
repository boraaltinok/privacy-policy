# Photo Editor - Support

Welcome to the Photo Editor support page. We're here to help you make the most of your photo editing experience.

## Getting Started

Photo Editor is a fast, simple photo editor that makes your photos look amazing in seconds. All editing — including AI-powered features — is performed entirely on your device for maximum privacy and speed. The app offers free basic editing tools and premium AI features through an optional subscription.

### Key Features
- **One-Tap Auto Enhance**: Instantly improve your photos with a single tap.
- **Photo Filters**: Apply beautiful filters to transform the look of your photos (5-8 free, 20+ with premium).
- **Manual Adjustments**: Fine-tune brightness, contrast, saturation, and sharpness with intuitive sliders.
- **AI Background Removal** (Premium): Remove backgrounds from your photos using on-device AI.
- **AI Background Blur** (Premium): Create professional depth-of-field blur effects.
- **Text Overlay**: Add custom text to your photos.
- **HD Export** (Premium): Save your edits in full resolution without watermarks.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General Questions

**Q: How do I get started with Photo Editor?**
A: Download the app, complete the brief onboarding, and tap "Select Photo" on the home screen to choose a photo from your gallery or take a new one with your camera.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes. All photo editing is performed entirely on your device — your photos are never uploaded to any server. We only collect anonymized usage analytics to improve the app. For more details, refer to our [Privacy Policy](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/boraaltinok/privacy-policy/main/photo-editor-privacy-policy.md).

**Q: Are my photos sent to the cloud?**
A: No. All processing — including AI background removal and blur — happens locally on your device. Your photos never leave your phone.

**Q: Who owns the photos I edit?**
A: You retain full ownership of all photos and edits you create. We do not claim any rights to your content. For further information, review our [Terms & Conditions](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/boraaltinok/privacy-policy/main/photo-editor-terms-conditions.md).

### Technical Support

**Q: The app is not working properly. What should I do?**
A: If you encounter issues, try these troubleshooting steps:
1. Close and restart the app.
2. Reboot your device.
3. Ensure you have the latest version of the app.
4. Check that you've granted camera and photo library permissions.
5. If a specific editing tool is slow, try working with a smaller image.

**Q: How do I update the app?**
A: App updates are typically automatic. You can also manually check for updates in the App Store.

**Q: Background removal isn't working well on my photo. What can I do?**
A: Background removal works best with:
- Clear contrast between subject and background
- Well-lit photos
- Single subjects (people, objects)

Try using a photo with better lighting or a more distinct background for improved results.

### Premium & Subscription

**Q: What features are included in the free version?**
A: The free version includes auto enhance, basic adjustments (brightness, contrast, saturation, sharpness), 5-8 filters, text overlay, and standard export (with watermark).

**Q: What do I get with a premium subscription?**
A: Premium unlocks AI background removal, AI background blur, all 20+ filters, HD/full-resolution export, and no watermark on exported photos.

**Q: How much does the premium subscription cost?**
A: We offer two plans:
- **Weekly:** $4.99/week
- **Yearly:** $39.99/year (includes a 3-day free trial)

**Q: How do I cancel my subscription?**
A: You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time through your device's Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions.

**Q: How do I request a refund?**
A: Refunds are handled by Apple. Visit [reportaproblem.apple.com](https://reportaproblem.apple.com) to request a refund.

## Account and Data Management

**Q: How do I delete my data?**
A: Since all photos and edits are stored locally on your device, you can delete them by removing the app or clearing app data. To request deletion of any analytics data associated with your anonymous user ID, contact us at [support@nextline.software](mailto:support@nextline.software).

**Q: Can I export my data?**
A: Your edited photos can be saved to your device's photo library or shared directly from the app. For analytics data export inquiries, contact our support team at [support@nextline.software](mailto:support@nextline.software).

**Q: How do I manage my subscription?**
A: Go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions on your device to view, change, or cancel your subscription.
