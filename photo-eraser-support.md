# Photo Eraser - Support

Welcome to the Photo Eraser support page. We're here to help you make the most of your photo editing experience.

## Getting Started

Photo Eraser is an AI-powered photo cleanup tool that removes unwanted objects and backgrounds from your photos. Background removal is performed entirely on your device for maximum privacy and speed. Object removal uses cloud-based AI processing for the best results. The app offers free daily removals and premium unlimited access through an optional subscription.

### Key Features
- **Object Removal**: Brush over unwanted objects and let AI erase them seamlessly using cloud-based inpainting.
- **Background Removal**: Remove photo backgrounds instantly using on-device AI processing.
- **HD Export** (Premium): Save your edits in full resolution without watermarks.
- **Recent Cleanups**: Access your recently edited photos for quick re-editing.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General Questions

**Q: How do I get started with Photo Eraser?**
A: Download the app, complete the brief onboarding, and tap on the home screen to select a photo from your gallery or take a new one with your camera. Then choose between object removal or background removal.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes. Background removal is performed entirely on your device — those photos are never uploaded to any server. Object removal requires secure cloud processing, but your images are not permanently stored on any server after processing. We only collect anonymized usage analytics to improve the app.

**Q: Are my photos sent to the cloud?**
A: It depends on the feature:
- **Background removal:** No. All processing happens locally on your device.
- **Object removal:** Yes. Your photo and brush mask are securely transmitted to our cloud service for AI processing, then the result is returned to your device. Images are not stored after processing.

**Q: Who owns the photos I edit?**
A: You retain full ownership of all photos and edits you create. We do not claim any rights to your content.

### Technical Support

**Q: The app is not working properly. What should I do?**
A: If you encounter issues, try these troubleshooting steps:
1. Close and restart the app.
2. Reboot your device.
3. Ensure you have the latest version of the app.
4. Check that you've granted camera and photo library permissions.
5. For object removal, ensure you have a stable internet connection.

**Q: How do I update the app?**
A: App updates are typically automatic. You can also manually check for updates in the App Store.

**Q: Object removal isn't working well on my photo. What can I do?**
A: Object removal works best when you:
- Brush carefully over the entire object you want to remove
- Use a larger brush for bigger objects
- Ensure the surrounding area has enough context for AI to fill in naturally
- Try with well-lit, high-contrast photos for best results

**Q: Background removal isn't clean around edges. What can I do?**
A: Background removal works best with:
- Clear contrast between subject and background
- Well-lit photos
- Single subjects (people, objects, animals)
- Distinct edges (avoid wispy hair or transparent objects)

## Account and Data Management

**Q: How do I delete my data?**
A: Since photos and edits are stored locally on your device, you can delete them by removing the app or clearing app data. To request deletion of any analytics data associated with your anonymous user ID, contact us at support@nextline.software.

**Q: Can I export my data?**
A: Your edited photos can be saved to your device's photo library or shared directly from the app. For analytics data export inquiries, contact our support team at support@nextline.software.

**Q: How do I manage my subscription?**
A: Go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions on your device to view, change, or cancel your subscription.
