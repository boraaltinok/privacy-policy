# Plant Identifier - Support

Welcome to the Plant Identifier support page. We're here to help you make the most of your experience with our application.

## Getting Started

Plant Identifier is an AI-powered mobile app that helps users identify plants instantly through photos and provides comprehensive care guidance. Users can capture or select plant images, receive detailed identification results with confidence scores, and access an intelligent AI assistant for personalized plant care advice. The app features a free tier with basic identification and limited AI assistant access, and a premium subscription that unlocks full care details, unlimited AI messages, and advanced growing tips. All plant images are stored locally on the device for privacy, with only base64-encoded images sent to our cloud AI service for identification. The app is available for iOS devices.

### Key Features
- **AI-powered Plant Identification From Photos**: Utilize AI technology to identify plants accurately from photos.
- **Camera Integration For Capturing Plant Images**: Seamlessly capture plant images within the app.
- **Comprehensive Care Guides (Light, Water, Soil, Temperature, Humidity, Fertilizer)**: Access detailed care instructions covering essential plant care aspects.
- **Plant Toxicity Information For Pets And Humans**: Stay informed about plant toxicity to ensure the safety of pets and humans.
- **AI Assistant For Plant Care Questions**: Get personalized plant care advice from the AI assistant.
