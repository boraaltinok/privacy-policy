## 1. Information Collection and Use

### 1.1 Automatically Collected Information
When you download and use the Application, certain data may be automatically collected, such as:
- **Device operating system and version**
- **Screens visited and time spent**
- **Crash logs and diagnostic data**
- **Anonymous usage statistics**

We use this data for analytics, troubleshooting, and improving the Application.

### 1.2 Location Data
The Application does **not** collect GPS-based or precise location information.

### 1.3 User-Provided Information
When you use certain features, we may collect:
- **Barcode scan data** — to look up product information
- **Product submissions** — product name and optional photo when you submit a product not found in our database
- **Support messages** — if you contact us via email

### 1.4 Local Device Data
The Application stores the following data locally on your device:
- **Scan history** — your previous scan results
- **Daily scan count** — to enforce the free tier scan limit
- **App preferences** — settings and onboarding status

This data is stored using Hive (local database) and does not leave your device unless you explicitly share it.

### 1.5 Barcode Scanning and Product Data
When you scan a barcode:
- The barcode number is sent to our server to retrieve product information
- Product data is sourced from public databases (Open Food Facts, Open Beauty Facts) and cached on our servers
- Scan telemetry (barcode, timestamp) is recorded for service improvement
- Scanned product results are also cached locally on your device

No images from your camera are transmitted during barcode scanning. Barcode detection occurs entirely on your device.

### 1.6 Product Submission Photos
If you submit a product photo:
- The photo is uploaded to Firebase Storage
- It is used solely to help identify and catalog the product
- Photos are associated with an anonymous user identifier

## 2. Anonymous Authentication
The Application uses Firebase Anonymous Authentication to generate a stable anonymous identifier for your device. This identifier:
- Does **not** require an email, password, or personal information
- Is used to associate your scan history and submissions
- Cannot be used to identify you personally

No login, registration, or account creation is involved.
