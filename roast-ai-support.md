## Getting Started

Roast AI turns a screenshot, a photo, or a quick description into **3 short, copyable roasts** in seconds.

### How to generate your first roast
1. Open Roast AI.
2. On the Home screen, choose one of the three modes:
   - **Upload Screenshot** — roast a chat, comment, post, or caption
   - **Roast a Photo** — roast a fit, background, or vibe
   - **Describe Someone** — type a short description (max 280 characters)
3. Pick a roast intensity: **Mild 🔥**, **Spicy 🔥🔥**, or **Savage 🔥🔥🔥**.
4. (Optional) Toggle **Gen-Z Mode** for sharper internet phrasing.
5. Tap **Roast it**.
6. Read the 3 roasts on the Result screen. Tap **Copy** or **Share** on any card. Tap **Generate 3 more** for a fresh batch.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Do I need to create an account?
No. Roast AI does not have a sign-up flow. An anonymous device account is created automatically the first time you open the app — no email, name, or password required.

### How many free roasts do I get?
Roast AI ships with a small **free tier (a lifetime cap on free generations)** so you can try the app before subscribing. Once your free generations are used up, the paywall appears the next time you tap **Roast it**.

### What does "1 generation = 3 roasts" mean?
Each time you tap **Roast it**, the app produces **3 roast outputs at once** for the same input. That counts as **one generation** against your free count or your subscription usage.

### Can I roast a photo of myself?
Yes. The roast is targeted at the **vibe, content, or context** of the input — not at any specific person's identity, face, or body. Roast AI does **not** perform facial recognition.

### Does Gen-Z Mode change the language?
Gen-Z Mode changes the **phrasing style**, not the language. Roasts are still written in the language matching your input or your device.

### What languages are supported?
The Application user interface is available in many languages. Roasts are generated by an AI model that supports a wide range of languages, including English, Turkish, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, and others. You can adjust language preferences in **Settings**.

### Can I share roasts on social media?
Yes. Tap the **Share** button on any roast card to open the system share sheet (Messages, social apps, etc.). Tap **Copy** to copy a single roast to your clipboard.

## Subscriptions & Billing

### What does the premium subscription unlock?
Premium unlocks **unlimited roast generations**. All input modes, the intensity selector, Gen-Z Mode, copy, and share are **never paywalled** — only the lifetime free-generation cap is removed.

### How do I subscribe?
Open the paywall (it appears when you tap **Roast it** after exhausting the free count, or from **Settings**) and choose a plan. Subscriptions are processed by **Apple via the App Store**.

### Is there a free trial?
Yes — the **6-Month plan** typically includes a **3-day free trial**. You will not be charged during the trial. If you do not cancel before the trial ends, the subscription converts to paid.

### How do I manage or cancel my subscription?
On iOS:
1. Open **Settings** on your device.
2. Tap your name at the top.
3. Tap **Subscriptions**.
4. Select **Roast AI** and tap **Cancel Subscription**.

You can also access this directly from the Roast AI in-app **Settings → Manage Subscription** link.

### How do I get a refund?
Refunds are handled by Apple. To request one:
1. Visit https://reportaproblem.apple.com on a browser.
2. Sign in with your Apple ID.
3. Find your Roast AI purchase and follow the prompts.

The Service Provider cannot directly process App Store refunds.

### I subscribed but the app still shows the paywall.
1. Open Roast AI and go to **Settings → Restore Purchases**.
2. Make sure you are signed into the same Apple ID that made the original purchase.
3. Ensure you have an internet connection.
4. If the issue persists, fully close and reopen the app.

If it still doesn't restore, email us at [support@nextline.software](mailto:support@nextline.software) with the purchase date and device model.

### Will the app sync subscriptions across devices?
Subscriptions are tied to your **Apple ID**. Sign into the same Apple ID on another device, install Roast AI, and use **Restore Purchases**.

## Troubleshooting

### The app is stuck on "Cooking 3 roasts…"
- Make sure you have a stable internet connection.
- Wait — the soft **Cancel** button appears after a few seconds if you'd like to abort.
- If a generation error appears, tap **Try again**. The failed attempt does **not** consume a free roast.

### "Image is too large" error
The Application compresses screenshots and photos to about 1024px on the longest edge. Very large source images may still exceed the size limit on rare devices. Try a smaller screenshot, or take a fresh, smaller photo.

### Photo picker shows "Permission Denied"
1. Open **Settings → Roast AI** on your device.
2. Tap **Photos**.
3. Choose **Selected Photos** or **All Photos** as preferred.
4. Return to Roast AI and try again.

### The app crashes when I open it
1. Force-close the app and reopen it.
2. Restart your device.
3. Update Roast AI to the latest version from the App Store.
4. If the crash persists, email us with your device model and iOS version.

### The roasts are in the wrong language
- Open **Settings** and check your **language mode** (Auto / Device / Manual).
- For text descriptions, the app uses your device language by default. For images, it tries to detect text in the image and match its language.

### The roast is offensive or refused to generate
- The AI provider applies safety filters and may refuse some inputs (for example, content targeting protected categories). When it refuses, the app shows a generation error rather than producing a roast.
- If a generated roast feels offensive in a way you'd like to flag, please email us with the screenshot and your input mode (no need to share the input itself if you prefer not to). We use this feedback to improve our prompts.

## Privacy & Content

### Are my screenshots, photos, or descriptions stored?
**No.** Your input is sent securely to the AI provider only at the moment you tap **Roast it**, used to generate the 3 roasts, and discarded. We do **not** store the input or the generated roasts on our servers. See the [Privacy Policy](roast-ai-privacy-policy.md) for full details.

### Does Roast AI use my location?
No. Roast AI does not request, collect, or use location data.

### Does Roast AI send notifications?
No. The current version does not send push notifications.

### Does Roast AI track me across other apps?
No. Roast AI does not show an App Tracking Transparency prompt because it does not perform cross-app tracking. Product analytics are anonymous and do not include the contents of your inputs or outputs.

### How do I delete my data?
You can:
1. **Uninstall the app** — this stops all collection from your device.
2. Optionally, email [support@nextline.software](mailto:support@nextline.software) and request deletion of your anonymous usage record. Because the account is anonymous, please mention your device model and approximate first-launch date so we can locate the record.
