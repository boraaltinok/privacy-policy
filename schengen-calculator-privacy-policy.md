# Privacy Policy — Schengen Calculator

**Last Updated:** July 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Schengen Calculator mobile application (the "Application") handles information. The Application is provided by NEXTLINE YAZILIM LİMİTED ŞİRKETİ (the "Service Provider") as a commercial service.

By using the Application, you agree to the practices described in this Policy.

---

## Summary

The Schengen Calculator is a **fully offline, on-device** tool. It helps non-EU travelers calculate their Schengen 90/180-day allowance. **Your trips, travel dates, traveller details, and passport/flag information never leave your device.** There are no accounts, no login, and no servers that store your travel data.

The only information that may ever leave your device is **anonymous, non-personal** diagnostic and usage data collected by third-party SDKs (analytics and crash reporting), and **subscription/purchase status** processed by our payments provider. None of this includes your trips or identity.

---

## Information You Enter (Stored On-Device Only)

To calculate your Schengen allowance, the Application lets you enter and save data such as:

- Trip entry and exit dates
- Traveller labels or names you choose
- Passport / flag selections and settings
- App preferences (theme, reminders, and similar options)

All of this information is stored **locally on your device** using an on-device database (Hive). It is **never transmitted to us or to any server**, and we cannot access it. This data remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the Application.

The Application performs all calculations (current status, "leave by" date, maximum future stay, and the rolling-window analysis) **entirely on your device**. No calculation requires an internet connection or a remote server.

---

## Information Collected Automatically (Anonymous)

The Application integrates third-party SDKs that may collect **anonymous, non-personal** technical information to help us understand app performance and improve the product. This may include:

- **Device and app information** — device model, operating system version, app version, language, and general region.
- **Usage events** — anonymous, aggregated interactions such as which screens are opened or which features are used.
- **Crash diagnostics** — anonymous crash logs and technical error reports used to diagnose and fix problems.

This automatically collected information is **pseudonymous** and is **not linked to your identity**. It **never includes** your trips, travel dates, traveller names, passport/flag data, or any content you enter into the Application.

The Application does **not** collect, request, or track the location of your device. It does not use GPS or any location services.
