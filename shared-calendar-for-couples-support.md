# Shared Calendar for Couples - Support

Welcome to the support page for **Shared Calendar for Couples** — the relationship-focused calendar app that helps you and your partner plan meaningful quality time together.

## Getting Started

Shared Calendar for Couples helps two connected partners move beyond schedule coordination to actively planning meaningful time together. After a short onboarding flow you can try Juno (the AI planning assistant) and save a date for free, then start a free trial when you are ready, sign in with Google or Apple, and connect with your partner using a 6-digit invitation code. Once connected, both partners share a calendar and can plan dates together.

### Key Features
- **Juno, your AI planning assistant**: chat with Juno for date ideas, planning advice, and personalized suggestions.
- **Shared Calendar**: a month view with all events created by either partner.
- **AI-Powered Date Ideas**: tap to receive personalized date suggestions based on your saved preferences.
- **Add and Edit Dates**: capture title, time, location, notes, an optional checklist, and photos.
- **Date Memories**: attach photos from your camera or photo library to any date.
- **Date Completion and Ratings**: mark dates complete and add a star rating with optional feedback.
- **Real-Time Partner Sync**: changes appear on your partner's device in seconds.
- **Local Reminders**: schedule device-local notifications for upcoming events.
- **Settings**: manage preferences, partner connection, and your account.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General

**Q: How do I get started?**
A: Download the Application, complete the onboarding flow, start the free trial when prompted, then sign in with Google or Apple. From the Welcome screen you can either generate a 6-digit code to invite your partner or enter the code your partner shared with you.

**Q: How do I connect with my partner?**
A: Open the Welcome / Partner screen. Tap **Generate Invitation Code** to create a 6-digit code and share it with your partner via your favorite messaging app. Your partner enters that code in the same screen during their onboarding to link your accounts.

**Q: My invitation code expired. What do I do?**
A: Invitation codes expire after 7 days. Generate a new code from the Partner screen and share it with your partner.

**Q: Can I connect with more than one partner?**
A: No. The Application is designed for one connected partner at a time.

**Q: How do I disconnect from my partner?**
A: Go to **Settings** and use the partner management option to disconnect. Disconnecting removes shared access going forward but does not delete the events that were already created.

### Subscriptions

**Q: Is there a free tier?**
A: Yes — a limited one. You can try Juno and save a date before subscribing so you can experience the core product. After that, unlocking the full app requires a subscription.

**Q: How does the free trial work?**
A: Subscribing may start an introductory free trial of the paid plan. The exact length is shown at checkout. You can cancel anytime before the trial ends to avoid being charged.

**Q: Why is there a paywall?**
A: The subscription keeps the app running — AI responses, cloud sync, and photo storage all cost real money per user. We offer a small free tier so you can try before you buy.

**Q: How do I cancel my subscription?**
A: Subscriptions are managed by Apple or Google. Open your device's **App Store / Play Store account → Subscriptions** to manage or cancel. Cancelling stops auto-renewal at the end of the current billing period. Deleting the Application does not cancel your subscription.

**Q: How do I restore a previous purchase?**
A: Open the paywall screen and tap **Restore Purchases**. RevenueCat will look up purchases tied to your App Store / Play Store account and restore your entitlement.

**Q: I was charged after cancelling — what should I do?**
A: Refunds are handled by Apple or Google. Use **Report a Problem** on Apple ([reportaproblem.apple.com](https://reportaproblem.apple.com)) or the **Refund Request** flow in your Google Play account. If you need additional context, email us.

### Partner & Calendar

**Q: My partner added an event but I can't see it.**
A: Try these steps:
1. Make sure both devices are online.
2. Pull to refresh on the Home, Calendar, or Ideas screen.
3. Confirm both accounts are still connected (Settings → Partner).
4. Close and reopen the Application.

**Q: Can we both edit the same event?**
A: Yes. Both connected partners can view, edit, and complete events on the shared calendar.

**Q: What happens to my events if I disconnect from my partner?**
A: Existing events remain in the shared calendar history but are no longer updated between accounts. Re-connecting with a new partner does not retroactively share past events.

### Juno (AI chat assistant)

**Q: What is Juno?**
A: Juno is the in-app AI planning assistant. You can chat with Juno to brainstorm date ideas, ask planning questions, or get personalized suggestions based on your preferences and recent dates.

**Q: How many messages do I get on the free tier?**
A: Free-tier users get a limited number of Juno messages so you can try the feature. Subscribing unlocks the full chat experience.

**Q: What information does Juno know about me?**
A: Juno receives a compact context each time you send a message: your first name, your partner's name (if set), your city and country, your saved date preferences, your chosen relationship-planning challenge, how many dates you have completed, and a short reference to your most recent date. Juno does **not** receive your email, sign-in identifiers, or payment details. See our [Privacy Policy §3](shared-calendar-for-couples-privacy-policy.md) for the full disclosure.

**Q: Is my chat with Juno visible to my partner?**
A: No. Juno conversations are private to you. Only events, notes, ratings, and photos you add to the shared calendar are visible to your connected partner.

**Q: Where are my chat messages stored?**
A: Chat messages are stored in our database under your account so your conversation history persists across your own devices. Deleting your account deletes your chat history.

**Q: Should I share sensitive information with Juno?**
A: Please don't. Juno is a planning assistant, not a secure channel. Avoid sending sensitive personal data (government IDs, health records, financial account numbers). Juno responses are generated by a statistical model and can be wrong.

### Date Suggestions (AI)

**Q: Where do the date suggestions come from?**
A: Suggestions are generated by a third-party AI inference service (**OpenRouter**) using a large language model. We send only your saved preferences and any optional free-text input you provide — never your email, sign-in identifier, or payment details.

**Q: Some suggestions don't match my preferences.**
A: AI suggestions are inspirational. Update your preferences in Settings, then request a new batch. You can also chat with Juno for more specific ideas.

**Q: Are suggestions accurate (e.g., real venues that are open)?**
A: No. AI suggestions are creative ideas only. Always verify that any specific venue, restaurant, or activity actually exists and is open before booking.

### Photos and Camera

**Q: How do I add photos to a date?**
A: Open a date from the calendar or details screen and tap the add-photo option. The Application will ask for your permission to access your camera and/or photo library the first time.

**Q: Where are my photos stored?**
A: Photos are uploaded to our secure cloud storage (Google Firebase) and stored in an isolated path accessible only to you and your connected partner. They are encrypted at rest and in transit.

**Q: Can my partner see the photos I add?**
A: Yes. Photos attached to shared dates are visible to both partners on the same couple account, just like event notes and checklist items.

**Q: I denied camera or photo library permission. How do I enable it later?**
A: Open your device settings:
- **iOS**: Settings → Privacy & Security → Camera / Photos → Shared Calendar for Couples
- **Android**: Settings → Apps → Shared Calendar for Couples → Permissions

You can always use the rest of the Application without granting photo access — you just won't be able to attach photos.

**Q: How do I delete a photo?**
A: Open the date from the Calendar or Home, find the photo in the memories section, and tap the delete icon.

### Notifications

**Q: How do reminders and notifications work?**
A: Two kinds of notifications:
- **Local reminders** — the Application schedules these on your device for upcoming events you have opted into. They don't need a server.
- **Remote push notifications** — delivered via Firebase Cloud Messaging (and Apple's push service on iOS) when your partner creates or updates a shared date, so you know something changed without opening the app.

**Q: I'm not getting any reminders.**
A: Make sure notifications are enabled for the Application:
- **iOS**: Settings → Notifications → Shared Calendar for Couples
- **Android**: Settings → Apps → Shared Calendar for Couples → Notifications

Then verify the relevant reminder toggle is on inside the Application's Settings.

### Account & Data

**Q: How do I delete my account?**
A: Open **Settings → Delete Account**, confirm twice, and your account, profile, preferences, chat history, uploaded photos, and personal data will be removed from our active systems. This action is permanent and cannot be undone. If you have an active subscription, cancel it separately through your **App Store / Play Store** to stop future charges.

**Q: What data does the app store about me?**
A: Account profile (email, display name, photo URL from your sign-in provider), onboarding preferences (first name, date types, relationship challenge), city and country derived from your device's language settings, partner connection details, events you create with your partner (title, time, location, notes, checklists, ratings, photos), your Juno chat history, and saved AI suggestions. See our [Privacy Policy](shared-calendar-for-couples-privacy-policy.md) for the full breakdown.

**Q: Can I export my data?**
A: For data export requests, please email us at [support@nextline.software](mailto:support@nextline.software).

### Troubleshooting

**Q: The app crashed or behaves unexpectedly.**
A: Try the following:
1. Close the Application completely and reopen it.
2. Restart your device.
3. Make sure you are running the latest version from the App Store / Play Store.
4. Confirm you have a stable internet connection.

If the problem persists, contact us with your device model, OS version, and a description of what you were doing when the issue occurred.

**Q: Can I use the app offline?**
A: Partially. You can view existing calendar events that have already synced to your device and read date details. Creating new events, generating AI suggestions, syncing with your partner, and accepting partner invitations all require an internet connection.
