## 2. AI Processing of Your Content

### 2.1 How AI Processing Works
The core functionality of the Application is powered by AI translation and understanding. To provide these features, content you create is sent securely via HTTPS to our backend (Firebase Cloud Functions) and forwarded to third-party AI providers for processing. Specifically:

- **Text you type** (for custom phrase cards and text translation) is sent to our backend and processed by a third-party large-language-model provider (OpenRouter) to generate a Thai translation and pronunciation.
- **Voice recordings** (for the Voice Translator feature) are sent to our backend, transcribed by OpenAI (Whisper), and translated by OpenRouter. Audio is recorded to a temporary file on your device and deleted after it is uploaded for processing.
- **Images you capture or select** (for the Scan & Ask feature) are compressed on your device, then sent to our backend and processed by OpenRouter's vision model to read and explain the Thai text they contain.

### 2.2 No Long-Term Server Storage of Your Content
Your text, voice recordings, and images are processed **on demand** to return a result and are **not stored on our servers as a personal record or history**. The only server-side caching is a short-lived technical cache used to avoid reprocessing an identical request; it expires automatically (within approximately 24 hours). We do **not** maintain a server-side history of your translations, scans, or recordings, and we do **not** offer cross-device sync.

### 2.3 Important Notice About Content You Submit
Images and voice recordings you submit may contain sensitive information such as personal documents, addresses, or private conversations. We recommend reviewing content before submitting it and avoiding capturing sensitive personal data that is not necessary for translation.

### 2.4 Server-Side Processing
AI processing (translation, transcription, and image understanding) is performed by our backend and the third-party providers listed in Section 6. Content is transmitted securely via HTTPS. Spoken Thai audio playback (text-to-speech) is generated entirely **on your device** using your device's built-in speech synthesis and is **not** sent to any server.

## 3. Anonymous Authentication

The Application uses **Firebase Anonymous Authentication** to create a session automatically upon first launch. This means:
- **No personal information** (name, email, password, phone number) is collected, and there is no login or user account to create.
- A unique anonymous identifier (UID) is generated and used to authorize requests to our backend and to associate analytics events.
- This anonymous UID is not linked to your real-world identity and is lost if you uninstall and reinstall the Application.

## 4. Analytics and Tracking

### 4.1 Analytics Services
The Application uses the following analytics services to understand usage patterns and improve the Application:

- **Google Analytics for Firebase**: Collects usage data, screen views, and app events. Data is processed by Google in accordance with their privacy policy.
- **Amplitude**: Collects session data, feature usage events, screen views, and custom events such as translation activity, paywall interactions, and error occurrences.

### 4.2 Events Tracked
The Application tracks the following types of events for analytics purposes:

### 4.3 Crash Reporting
The Application uses **Firebase Crashlytics** to record crashes and errors. Crash reports may include diagnostic information such as device model, operating system version, and technical stack traces. They do **not** include the content you submit for translation.

### 4.4 App Tracking Transparency (iOS)
On iOS devices, the Application may request permission to track your activity through the App Tracking Transparency framework. You may grant or deny this permission, and you can change it at any time in your device settings. Denying tracking does not affect the core functionality of the Application.
