# Support

Welcome to support for **Thai Translator: Talk & Travel**. This page answers common questions and explains how to get help.

---

## Getting Started

Thai Translator: Talk & Travel helps you speak, scan, and understand Thai while traveling. With it, you can:

- **Scan & Ask** — Photograph a Thai menu, sign, or label to have it read and explained, then ask follow-up questions about it.
- **Voice Translator** — Speak in your language and get the Thai translation with pronunciation, or understand spoken Thai.
- **Custom AI Cards** — Type any phrase in your language and instantly create a Thai phrase card with pronunciation.
- **Built-in Phrase Cards & Show Mode** — Browse ready-made travel phrases, hear them spoken aloud, and show them full-screen to the person you're talking to.

No account or sign-up is required. Just open the app and start.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General

**Do I need an account to use the app?**
No. The app works without any login or sign-up. It creates an anonymous session automatically so you can start using it right away.

**Which permissions does the app need, and why?**
- **Camera** — to scan Thai menus, signs, and labels.
- **Microphone** — to record your voice for the Voice Translator.
- **Photo Library** — to choose an existing photo of Thai text to scan, or to save images.
- **Notifications** (optional) — for helpful reminders.

You can grant or deny each permission, and change it later in your device settings. The app asks for a permission only when you first use the feature that needs it.

**Does the app work offline?**
The built-in phrase cards and spoken audio playback work offline. The AI features — Scan & Ask, Voice Translator, and Custom AI Cards — require an internet connection because the translation and image understanding are processed online.

### Translation Accuracy

**How accurate are the translations?**
Translations, transcriptions, and explanations are generated by AI and are designed to help you communicate while traveling. They may occasionally contain errors. Please do not rely on the app for critical, medical, legal, or safety-related decisions.

**Why do some phrases have a male/female speaking style?**
Thai politeness particles differ by speaker. You can choose a male, female, or neutral speaking style in settings so phrases match how you'd like to speak.

### History and Data

**Where are my custom cards and history stored?**
Your custom cards, recent items, and preferences are stored **only on your device**. They are not synced to a server or to other devices.

**Will I lose my cards if I reinstall the app?**
Yes. Because your data is stored locally on your device, uninstalling or reinstalling the app permanently removes your custom cards and history.

### Subscriptions and Billing

**What's included for free?**
You get a limited number of uses of the AI features (scans, voice translations, and custom cards), plus unlimited use of the built-in phrase cards and audio playback.

**What does premium unlock?**
A premium subscription removes the free-tier limits and gives you unlimited use of the AI features.

**How do I restore my subscription?**
If you previously subscribed, use the "Restore Purchases" option on the paywall or in settings to restore your premium access on the same Apple ID.

**How do I manage or cancel my subscription?**
Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. Open the App Store, tap your profile, then **Subscriptions** to view, change, or cancel. You can also reach this from the app's settings.

**How do refunds work?**
Purchases are handled by Apple. Refund requests are subject to Apple's policies and must be made through Apple.

### Technical Support

**A feature isn't working — what should I try?**
1. Check that you have an active internet connection (required for AI features).
2. Make sure the relevant permission (camera, microphone, or photos) is granted in your device settings.
3. Close and reopen the app.
4. Make sure you're running the latest version from the App Store.
