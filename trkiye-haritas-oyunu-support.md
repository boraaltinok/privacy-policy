## Getting Started

Türkiye Haritası Oyunu is an engaging app that offers various features to help you learn about Turkey's provinces, regions, geographic features, and cultural heritage. The app is available for iOS devices.

### Key Features
- **Interactive Svg Turkey Map For Location Testing**: Test your knowledge with an interactive SVG Turkey map.
- **Random City And Region Quiz Questions**: Engage with random city and region quiz questions.
- **Geographic Features Flashcards**: Study provinces, rivers, lakes, mountains, regions, and climate with flashcards.
- **Cultural Information Flashcards**: Explore local cuisine, festivals, historical sites, famous people, crafts, and natural landmarks.
- **Ai-powered Geography Tutor In Turkish**: Receive personalized help from an AI geography tutor.
- **Simple Progress Tracking And Scoring System**: Monitor your progress easily.
- **Flashcard Categories**: Access flashcards under Geographic and Cultural categories.
- **Difficulty Levels**: Choose between Easy, Medium, and Hard difficulty levels.
- **Daily Question Limits For Engagement**: Stay engaged with daily question limits.
- **Favorite Flashcards Feature**: Mark your favorite flashcards.
- **Offline Capability**: Use all features offline except the AI tutor.
