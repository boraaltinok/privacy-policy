## Getting Started

Ukulele Tuner is designed to help beginning ukulele players tune their instrument with confidence. The app provides real-time pitch detection and clear visual feedback to make tuning stress-free and immediate.

### Key Features
- **Real-time Tuning**: Advanced audio processing for accurate pitch detection
- **Visual Feedback**: Clear Red → Yellow → Green status indicators
- **Ukulele-Specific**: Optimized for standard ukulele tuning (G-C-E-A)
- **Beginner-Friendly**: Simple interface without technical complexity
- **Multiple Tuning Types**: Support for Standard, Low G, and Baritone tunings
- **Haptic Feedback**: Gentle vibration when tuning is perfect

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General Questions

**Q: How do I get started with Ukulele Tuner?**  
A: Download the app, complete the brief onboarding process, grant microphone permission, and start tuning! Simply pluck any ukulele string and follow the color-coded feedback.

**Q: Which ukulele types are supported?**  
A: The app supports Soprano, Concert, Tenor, and Baritone ukuleles with multiple tuning options including Standard tuning, Low G tuning, and Baritone tuning.

**Q: Do I need an internet connection?**  
A: No! Ukulele Tuner works completely offline. All audio processing happens on your device, so you can tune anywhere without internet.

**Q: Is my audio data secure?**  
A: Absolutely. The app processes audio in real-time for pitch detection but never records, stores, or transmits any audio data. All tuning analysis happens locally on your device.

### Technical Support

**Q: The app isn't detecting my ukulele strings. What should I do?**  
A: Try these troubleshooting steps:
1. Ensure microphone permission is granted in your device settings
2. Play in a quiet environment to reduce background noise
3. Pluck strings firmly and clearly
4. Make sure your device's microphone isn't blocked
5. Try restarting the app

**Q: The tuning seems inaccurate. How can I improve it?**  
A: For best results:
- Use the app in a quiet room
- Hold your ukulele close to your device's microphone
- Pluck strings individually, not as chords
- Ensure your ukulele strings are in reasonable condition
- Try adjusting the microphone sensitivity in settings if available

**Q: The app crashes or freezes. What should I do?**  
A: If you encounter stability issues:
1. Force close and restart the app
2. Restart your device
3. Ensure you have the latest app version from the App Store
4. Check that your device has sufficient available storage
5. Contact support if the problem persists

**Q: How do I update the app?**  
A: App updates are typically automatic. You can also manually check for updates in the App Store by searching for "Ukulele Tuner" and tapping "Update" if available.
